## Advanced Tips for New Agents in 2026

If you are just starting your journey or returning for a new season, keep these expert strategies in mind to maximize your efficiency:

*   **Follow the Exclamation Points:** The UI can be overwhelming with sub-menus. Always click through the red exclamation points; these often lead to milestone rewards, Phoenix Credits, and specialization points that are easy to miss.
*   **Match Damage Types:** As you reach level 20 and beyond, enemies will spawn with specific elemental shields. Always keep one weapon of each damage type (Blast, Shredding, and Piercing) in your inventory to avoid hitting a progression wall.
*   **Prioritize Daily Quests:** Because of the daily XP cap, your first hour of play is your most productive. Complete your daily objectives and the 10 rewarded open-world activities first before grinding nests or the Dark Zone.
*   **Server Selection:** Remember that servers (Americas, Europe, Asia) are isolated. If you plan to play with friends, ensure everyone selects the same region at the start, as characters cannot be transferred between them.

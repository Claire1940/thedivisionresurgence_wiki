## Maximizing Your Progression in 2026

The Division 2 has become highly event-centric. Instead of relying solely on codes, players should align their playtime with the active event calendar. ### Top Farming Methods for Returning Players
If you are looking to catch up to the current power level, focus on these activities:

1.  **SHD Level Grinding:** Set your world difficulty to Challenging or Heroic and enable as many directives as you can handle. Focus on **Public Executions** and **Propaganda Broadcasts** in the open world. These offer the highest XP-to-time ratio.
2.  **Expertise Farming:** Equip as many "non-proficient" items as possible. Head into the **Countdown** game mode on Challenging difficulty. Set your targeted loot to the category you need (e.g., Shotguns) and loot everything.
3.  **The Junk Donation Strategy:** After a Countdown run, use the "Donate All Junk" button at the Expertise bench. This is the fastest way to level up your gear proficiency without spending rare resources.

> **💡 Tip:** Retaliations are currently the best way to farm for high-end and exotic blueprints that were previously locked behind difficult raids or rare world drops.

### Safe House Hidden Vaults

Safe houses provide sanctuary, but they also hide secrets. In 2026, exploring every corner of these "green zones" is the best way to find high-tier loot without engaging in combat.

*   **Autumn's Hope Safe House:** Locate the reception desk behind the NPC Mullins. Look for the "Established" year on the wall. The code is **1883**.
*   **Dante's Run Safe House:** Exit the safe house and head toward the mannequin in the clothing store. Behind the desk, you will find a safe. The code, found on a nearby poster, is **7716**.
*   **Kerman Station:** Head toward the lockers near the exit. The code is pinned directly next to the lockers: **7069**.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are having trouble seeing the numbers on posters or signs, try increasing your "Object Detail" in the graphics settings temporarily. Some clues are resolution-dependent!

## Understanding the Cryptic Clue System

In *The Division Resurgence*, safes are not just random boxes; they are tied to the "Cryptic Clues" section of your collectibles menu. There are 11 total clues to find, and **the division resurgence code clue 10** is often one of the final hurdles for completionists. Unlike standard loot crates, these safes require a four-digit or three-digit combination that is hidden somewhere in the immediate environment.

Clues can appear in various forms:
*   **Chalkboards:** Often found in settlements or classrooms.
*   **Notice Boards:** Look for pinned notes in dormitories or safe houses.
*   **Street Signs:** Check shop windows or "Established Since" dates on building plaques.
*   **Collectibles:** Some codes are only revealed after picking up a specific audio log or electronic device.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Some safes are guarded by elite enemies or located in contaminated zones. Ensure your filter level is sufficient before attempting to reach safes in the Dark Zone or high-threat districts.

## Detailed Walkthroughs for Major Safes

### Base of Operations Safes
The Base of Operations (BoO) is a hub of activity, but many agents run right past the three safes hidden in its immediate vicinity. After fast-traveling to the BoO, head toward the dormitories to find the first safe. The code **1350** is clearly marked on a notice board nearby.

For the second and third safes, exit the main entrance and take a right. You will find the Oak Lock store and a supermarket across the street. These are excellent for early-game gear boosts.

### The Serotentia "Birthday Gift" Puzzle
This is widely considered one of the more "cryptic" clues in the game. Located near the old Madison Field Hospital (a callback to the original *The Division*), you will find a large safe inside the Serotentia building. 
1.  Locate the collectible on the ground outside the entrance.
2.  Read the clue: "Birthday Gift."
3.  The hint points to November 5th or May 11th.
4.  Input **1105** to unlock the safe.

> **💡 Tip:** If 1105 does not work, try 0511. Regional date formats sometimes affect how puzzles are interpreted in different versions of the game.

## How to Track Your Cryptic Clues

If you are unsure if you have already found **the division resurgence code clue 10**, you can check your progress in the main menu:

## FAQ

**Q: Where is the division resurgence code clue 10 located?**

A: Clue 10 is part of the Cryptic Clues collectible set. It is typically found in the later districts like Murray Hill or Turtle Bay. Most players associate it with the "Birthday Gift" safe or the Kerman Station lockers.

**Q: Do the safe codes change for different players?**

A: No, the safe codes in *The Division Resurgence* are static. They are based on environmental clues that remain the same for every agent. However, loot inside is randomized based on your current level.

**Q: Can I open a safe more than once?**

A: No, these specific "clue" safes are one-time rewards per character. Once opened, they will remain open, though you can still view the clues in your collectibles menu.

**Q: What should I do if the code 1105 doesn't work for the Birthday Gift safe?**

A: Ensure you have picked up the collectible item first. Sometimes the safe interaction won't trigger correctly until the game registers that you have "found" the clue. If it still fails, try restarting your session to refresh the world state.

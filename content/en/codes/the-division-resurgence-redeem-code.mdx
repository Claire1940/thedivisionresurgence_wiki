## Maximizing Daily Progression

The Division Resurgence utilizes a "Daily XP Boost" system, indicated by an orange arrow near your level bar. When this arrow is lit, you gain XP at a significantly accelerated rate. Once the arrow turns gray, you have hit your daily "soft cap," and XP gains will slow down.

To make the most of your active boost, prioritize these activities in order:
1.  **Daily Quests:** These provide the highest XP-to-time ratio and often reward Phoenix Credits.
2.  **Main Campaign:** Save these for when your orange boost is active to jump multiple levels at once.
3.  **Nests:** These are instanced combat zones. Keep an eye out for "Heroic Nests," which stay active for 24 hours and offer the best loot drops.
4.  **Open World Activities:** You can receive rewards from up to 10 of these per day. Fast travel is available once an activity is discovered.

> **Warning:** Crafting in Resurgence is governed by real-time timers (often 24 hours). Avoid spending rare materials on low-tier crafting unless required by a mission objective.

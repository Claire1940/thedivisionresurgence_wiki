## Understanding the Cryptic Clue System

In *The Division Resurgence*, safes are not just random loot boxes; they are part of a structured collectible system. When you approach a locked safe, your ISAC system will often announce an "Object of Interest detected." To open these, you must find a nearby clue. These clues are usually integrated into the world-building elements of the environment.

The game tracks these through the "Cryptic Clues" tab in your collectibles menu. There are 11 primary clues to find in total. While some are straightforward numbers written on walls, others require you to interpret dates or specific historical facts mentioned in nearby documents.

| Clue Type | Visual Indicator | Typical Location |
|---|---|---|
| **Chalkboard** | Handwritten white text | Settlements and classrooms |
| **Notice Board** | Pinned paper or flyers | Base of Operations and Safe Houses |
| **Signage** | "Established" dates or prices | Storefronts and supermarkets |
| **Collectibles** | Glowing blue icons on floor | Near "Big Safes" in mission areas |

> **Warning:** Some codes are region-specific or may require you to pick up a specific collectible item before the safe becomes interactable. Always check your surroundings for blue glowing icons first.

## Primary Safe Locations and Codes

To maximize your efficiency, follow these specific routes to the most rewarding safes currently available in the 2026 build of the game.

### Spring Blossom Settlement Safe
This is usually the first safe agents encounter. Upon fast traveling to the Spring Blossom Settlement, head to the upper levels of the main building. You will find a room containing a small black safe.

*   **Clue Location:** Look at the chalkboard immediately to the right of the safe.
*   **Code:** **3580**
*   **Reward:** Typically yields one Superior (Purple) item and basic electronics.

### NYC Base of Operations: Dormitories
Inside the main Base of Operations (BoO), navigate toward the dormitory section where agents rest. 

*   **Clue Location:** A small slip of paper is pinned to the notice board just before you reach the safe.
*   **Code:** **1350**

### The Oaklock Store (Near BoO)
Exit the Base of Operations and take an immediate right. Follow the street down until you see the "Oaklock" storefront on your left. The safe is sitting on the floor behind the main counter.

*   **Clue Location:** A promotional sign near the entrance.
*   **Code:** **122**

### The Supermarket (Across from Oaklock)
Directly across the street from the Oaklock store is a large supermarket. Enter the building and head toward the back wall.

*   **Clue Location:** Look for a large hanging sign or poster above the refrigerated section.
*   **Code:** **1987**

## Advanced Riddles: The Birthday Gift

One of the more complex puzzles involves a larger safe located near the Madison Field Hospital area (a callback to the original *Division*). This safe is tied to a specific collectible titled "Birthday Gift." Unlike simpler safes, you must pick up the physical clue from the ground before you can even attempt to input the code.

The clue mentions a specific date: "Tuesday 11th of May" or "5th of November." For players tracking **code clue 10 the division**, this logic-based puzzle is a prime example of how the game hides combinations.

## Safe House Hidden Stashes

Safe houses serve as your primary fast travel hubs, but many players overlook the loot hidden right under their noses. As you unlock new districts in 2026, make sure to check the following safe houses.

### Autumn's Hope
Located in the northern district, this safe house has a safe right next to the reception desk where Mullins is stationed.

*   **Clue Location:** The "Established" year on the sign behind the reception desk. Note that depending on your graphics settings, the numbers might be slightly blurred.
*   **Code:** **1883**

### Dante's Run
After exiting the safe house through the main door, take a right past the clothing mannequin. You will enter a small retail area.

*   **Clue Location:** A poster on the front of the checkout desk.
*   **Code:** **7716**

### Kerman Station
This is often the final safe players find in the mid-game transition. Exit the spawn room and look for the "Safia Nerdies" lockers.

*   **Clue Location:** A handwritten note pinned to the side of the locker unit.
*   **Code:** **7069**

> **Tip:** If a code doesn't work, ensure you have actually "inspected" the clue in the environment. Sometimes the safe interaction is locked until the ISAC system registers the clue.

## Critical Early Game Decisions

Before you start hunting for **the division resurgence code** locations, you must navigate the initial setup. The choices you make in the first ten minutes of gameplay have lasting impacts on your ability to play with friends and your general combat efficiency.

### Server Selection and Crossplay
The Division Resurgence features full crossplay between iOS, Android, and PC emulators. However, servers are strictly isolated. If you choose the American server and your friend chooses the European server, you cannot group up. Always coordinate with your clan before finalizing your character creation.

### Specializations and Classes
At the end of the tutorial, you will pick a Specialization. Unlike the rigid classes in other RPGs, Resurgence allows you to visit the Coordinator at the Base of Operations to switch specializations relatively early. If you feel your current build isn't "gelling," check the Specialization Guide in your menu for class-specific objectives that teach you the optimal skill rotations.

## Gear Level vs. Power Score

One of the biggest departures from the mainline console games is how gear handles progression. In Resurgence, your gear has an independent level that can be increased alongside your character. This means a high-end backpack found at level 6 can be upgraded all the way to level 40 if the stats and talents are worth keeping.

**Power Score** is the ultimate metric of your readiness. It is influenced by:
*   The raw level and rarity of your weapons and armor.
*   The level of mods equipped on your gear.
*   Account-wide achievements and "Agent Career" milestones.
*   Specialization point allocation.

Matching these damage types becomes mandatory in the late-game (levels 20+), as bosses will spawn with specific elemental shields that can only be broken by the corresponding weapon type.

## Optimizing Daily Progression

To prevent player burnout and maintain a balanced economy, Resurgence implements a daily XP system. Understanding the "Orange Arrow" is key to efficient leveling.

*   **The XP Boost:** Look at the orange arrow near your character level. If it is lit up, you are gaining full XP. Once it turns gray, your XP gains are significantly reduced for the remainder of the day.
*   **Open World Limits:** You can only receive full rewards from 10 open-world activities per daily reset. Plan your route to hit the most rewarding activities (like Nests) first.
*   **Fast Travel:** Once you discover an open-world activity, you can fast travel directly to it from the map, saving you minutes of running through the streets.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not save side missions for the endgame. Unlike The Division 2, side mission XP in Resurgence is locked to the level you received the mission at. Complete them as you find them for the best benefit.

## FAQ

**Q: Where do I enter a promo the division resurgence code for free items?**

A: Promo codes (Redeem Codes) are usually entered through the "Settings" menu under the "Account" or "Basic" tab. Look for a button labeled "Redeem" or "Gift Code." Note that these are different from the physical safe codes found in the game world.

**Q: Can I share gear between my different characters?**

A: No. In The Division Resurgence, purchases and gear are currently character-bound. This makes the initial choice of specialization and server even more important, as progress does not easily transfer.

**Q: What happens if I dismantle an upgraded item?**

A: You will receive 90% of the materials you invested back into your inventory. This allows you to pivot to new gear without losing your entire investment, though there is still a 10% "tax" on resources.

**Q: How do I find the combination for a safe if it isn't on a wall?**

A: Check your "Database" menu under the "Cryptic Clues" tab. Many safe combinations are hidden within collectible text files you pick up in the immediate area of the locked box.

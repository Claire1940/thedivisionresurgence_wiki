## Solving Cryptic Clues and Code Clue 10

As you progress through the main campaign, you will unlock a specific section in your "Collectibles" menu titled **Cryptic Clues**. There are 11 of these in total. These are not just lore entries; they are direct keys to the most well-hidden safes in the game.

The **code clue 10 division resurgence** entry is often one of the last ones players find. These clues usually follow a pattern:
1.  **Identify the Collectible**: Look for a golden or blue glow on the ground in non-combat areas.
2.  **Read the Text**: Open your inventory and read the clue carefully. It will often mention a year, a birthday, or an anniversary.
3.  **Format the Code**: Most codes are four digits. If a clue says "May 12th," the code is likely **0512**.

> **💡 Tip:** If you find a safe but cannot find the clue, check the "Cryptic Clues" tab in your menu. You might have already picked up the solution without realizing it.

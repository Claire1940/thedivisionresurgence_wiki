## Core Mechanics of Outpost Annihilation

The primary objective in an **outpost annihilation** mission is the total systemic collapse of an enemy's fortified position. Unlike standard skirmishes, these missions are divided into distinct phases: the Infiltration Phase, the Siege Phase, and the final Annihilation Phase. During the final stage, the enemy will trigger an "Overdrive" protocol, sending waves of elite units to protect their core.

To succeed, you must focus on structural weak points. Most outposts in 2026 feature modular armor plating that is resistant to small arms fire but vulnerable to corrosive or explosive damage. Identifying these nodes early in the assault can save you thousands of credits in wasted ammunition.

| Structure Type | Vulnerability | Priority Level | Recommended Weapon |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shield Generators** | Ion/Electric | **Critical** | EMP Grenade / Railgun |
| **Automated Turrets** | Kinetic/Armor Piercing | **High** | Sniper Rifle / Anti-Material |
| **Command Core** | Explosive/Thermal | **Objective** | C4 / Rocket Launcher |
| **Repair Drones** | High-Frequency Kinetic | **Medium** | LMG / Assault Rifle |

> **💡 Tip:** Always disable the Repair Drone hubs before focusing on the Command Core. If left active, drones can restore up to 15% of the core's health every thirty seconds.

## Step-by-Step Assault Strategy

Successfully clearing a fortified zone requires more than just raw power. Follow this tactical sequence to optimize your efficiency and minimize casualties.

### 1. Reconnaissance and Marking
Before firing a single shot, use your drone or binoculars to mark all visible turrets and generator nodes. In **outpost annihilation**, the fog of war is your greatest enemy. Knowing exactly where the enemy's line of sight begins allows you to position your mobile outpost just outside their effective range.

### 2. The Kinetic Breach
Use long-range anti-material rifles to pick off external sensors. Once the sensors are down, the outpost's automated defenses will have a 50% slower acquisition time. This is your window to move in your heavy vehicles or deploy your primary breach charges.

### 3. Neutralizing the Core
Once you have entered the inner sanctum, the **outpost annihilation** timer will begin. You will be swarmed by high-tier infantry. Use area-of-effect (AoE) weapons like incendiary grenades to create "kill zones" at the entrance of the core room while your specialist focuses on the objective.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not stand near the Command Core when its health reaches 5%. It will often trigger a self-destruct sequence that deals massive damage to anyone within a 10-meter radius.

## Resource Management and Loot Optimization

Every **outpost annihilation** attempt consumes significant resources, including power cells, ammo crates, and nanites for repairs. To ensure your campaign remains profitable, you must balance your consumption with the loot gathered from the wreckage.

1.  **Scavenge Before Extraction:** After the "Mission Complete" banner appears, you usually have a three-minute window before extraction. Use this time to deconstruct enemy turrets for rare components.
2.  **Use Energy Weapons:** While they require frequent cooling, energy weapons do not consume physical ammo, saving you thousands of credits over the course of a long mission.
3.  **Upgrade Your Cargo Cap:** You cannot profit from an **outpost annihilation** if you cannot carry the loot home. Invest in high-capacity storage modules for your mobile base early in the game.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to complete an outpost annihilation mission?**

A: The fastest method involves using a "Stealth Breach" strategy. By using silenced weapons to take out generators without alerting the main garrison, you can plant explosives on the Command Core and trigger the annihilation phase before the heavy reinforcements even spawn.

**Q: Can I play outpost annihilation solo in 2026?**

A: Yes, it is possible, but highly difficult. You will need to rely heavily on automated drones and "distraction" decoys to manage the enemy's attention. Solo players should focus on high-mobility builds rather than heavy tanking.

**Q: Why is my damage so low against the Command Core?**

A: You are likely using standard kinetic ammunition. Command Cores in **outpost annihilation** modes are typically protected by "Composite Plating," which reduces standard bullet damage by 80%. Switch to High-Explosive (HE) rounds or Thermal lasers to bypass this resistance.

**Q: Does the difficulty scale with the number of players?**

A: Yes, the game scales the health of the structures and the number of elite defenders based on your squad size. However, the loot quality also increases significantly with more players, making four-man squads the most efficient way to farm rare materials.

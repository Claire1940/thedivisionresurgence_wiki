## A-Tier: Reliable Alternatives for End-Game Content

While S-Tier weapons are the goal, A-Tier options are highly effective and can easily carry an agent through World Tier 5 content and most Incursions. These guns often have one minor drawback—such as slightly lower fire rates or smaller magazines—but remain top-tier choices for specific builds.

*   **ACR (Assault Rifle):** Offers handling that rivals the Lightweight M4 but suffers from a slightly lower rate of fire. It is an excellent choice for players who prefer laser-like precision over raw burst speed.
*   **Vector 45 ACP (SMG):** Famous for its blistering fire rate, the Vector can melt enemies at close range instantly. However, its small magazine requires frequent reloading, making it less forgiving in prolonged firefights.
*   **Custom M44 (Marksman Rifle):** The preferred choice for "one-tap" builds in the Dark Zone. While it lacks the utility of the M700 Carbon, its raw bolt-action stopping power is unmatched.
*   **Big Alejandro (LMG):** An exotic light machine gun that excels in defensive play. Its unique talent increases fire rate and damage while firing from cover, making it the king of suppression.

## Understanding Weapon Talents and Stats

In the world of **ディビジョン リサージェンス 武器**, the base damage of a gun is only half the story. To truly reach the top of the leaderboards, your weapon's talents must synergize with your core stats: Firearms, Toughness, and Electronics. A weapon with slightly lower base damage but perfect talents will almost always outperform a "plain" high-damage drop.

### Essential PvE Talents for 2026
For those focusing on Incursions like "Dragon's Nest" or "Falcon Lost," these talents are non-negotiable for efficiency:
1.  **Destructive:** Increases enemy armor damage. Since almost all end-game enemies are heavily armored, this is a mandatory stat.
2.  **Ferocious:** Boosts damage against Elite and Named enemies, which is vital for boss encounters.
3.  **Predatory:** Killing a target regenerates health over time. This provides essential sustain for solo players or those running high-DPS glass cannon builds.

### Best PvP Talents for the Dark Zone
When entering the Dark Zone, the priority shifts toward burst damage and utility to win quick 1v1 or 4v4 skirmishes.

| Talent | Effect | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Responsive** | Damage increases when close to the target | Essential for SMG rushers and shotgun-heavy builds. |
| **Unforgiving** | Damage increases as your health drops | A high-risk, high-reward talent that often decides the winner of a close duel. |
| **Competent** | Weapon damage increases after using a skill | Pairs perfectly with Pulse or First Aid for a sudden damage spike. |

## Class-Specific Meta Analysis

### Assault Rifles (AR)
Assault Rifles remain the most popular choice for **ディビジョン リサージェンス 武器** due to their innate "Enemy Armor Damage" bonus. The **Lightweight M4** continues to sit on the throne, but for players who can manage high horizontal recoil, the **Bullfrog** (Exotic) offers a compelling alternative with its unique damage-scaling talent based on your weapon's base stability.

### Submachine Guns (SMG)
SMGs are defined by their high critical hit chance. This makes them the primary pairing for "Striker" or "D3-FNC" gear sets. While **The House** is the undisputed champion, the **Aug A3** is a very stable alternative for players who find the Vector's recoil too erratic for mobile play.

### Marksman Rifles (Sniper)
If you prefer the "Sentry’s Call" or "DeadEYE" playstyles, the only snipers worth considering for serious end-game play are the **M700 Carbon** and the **Custom M44**. These weapons scale the best with high Firearms stats, allowing for massive headshot multipliers.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid using high-capacity "spam" snipers like the SCAR-H in PvP. They generally lack the burst damage required to down an enemy before they can use a medkit or hide behind cover.

## How to Farm Top-Tier Weapons in 2026

Acquiring the best **ディビジョン リサージェンス 武器** requires a strategic approach to farming. Since the March 31 release, several high-efficiency methods have emerged for players looking to optimize their gear score:

*   **Global Events:** These monthly events allow you to earn GE Tokens, which can be traded for Classified Gear Caches and Exotic Weapon Caches. This is the most reliable way to target specific exotics like "The House."
*   **Legendary Missions:** Completing missions such as "Amherst's Apartment" on Legendary difficulty guarantees an exotic drop once per week. These are challenging but essential for end-game progression.
*   **Resistance Mode:** This horde-based mode is widely considered the best way to farm for "God Roll" weapons. By opening the reward crates after Wave 10, players have a significantly higher chance of finding top-tier items.
*   **The Dark Zone:** Areas DZ07 through DZ09 offer the highest density of high-end drops. However, you must successfully extract your loot, which carries the risk of rogue agents stealing your hard-earned gear.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the single best weapon in Division Resurgence right now?**

A: While it depends on your build, the **Lightweight M4** is widely considered the most versatile weapon due to its high fire rate and predictable recoil. For SMG enthusiasts, **The House** is the undisputed king of burst damage.

**Q: How do I re-roll bad talents on my weapons?**

A: Visit the Recalibration Station at the Base of Operations. You can spend Weapon Parts and Phoenix Credits to change one talent on a high-end weapon. Note that once you change one talent, the others become locked, so choose wisely.

**Q: Is Gear Score more important than weapon talents?**

A: No. A lower Gear Score weapon with perfect talents (like Destructive and Ferocious) will almost always outperform a max Gear Score weapon with useless talents. You can eventually use the Optimization Station to raise the Gear Score of your "God Roll" weapon to the maximum level.

**Q: Can I get Exotic weapons outside of the Dark Zone?**

A: Absolutely. In the 2026 meta, exotics can be obtained from Weekly Caches, Legendary Mission rewards, Resistance crates, and even Open World bosses. The Dark Zone is simply one of the fastest—and most dangerous—ways to farm for **ディビジョン リサージェンス 武器**.

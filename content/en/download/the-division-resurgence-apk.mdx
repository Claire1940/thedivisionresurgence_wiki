## The Dark Zone: High Stakes PvPvE

No Division experience is complete without the Dark Zone (DZ). This is a walled-off section of Manhattan where the virus is most concentrated and the rules of engagement are non-existent. In the DZ, you will face the toughest AI enemies for the best possible loot, but there is a catch: other players can turn "Rogue" and steal your gear.

Any loot found in the Dark Zone is contaminated and must be extracted via helicopter. Once you call for an extraction, every player in the vicinity is alerted. This creates the tense, heart-pounding moments the series is famous for. You must decide whether to trust the agents who show up at your extraction point or prepare for a firefight.

> **Warning:** Going Rogue will mark you on the map for all nearby agents. While the rewards for successful Rogue actions are high, the risk of losing all your progress in that session is equally great.

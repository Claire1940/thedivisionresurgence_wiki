## FAQ

**Q: Can I play The Division Resurgence offline to save data?**

A: No, The Division Resurgence is a persistent online open-world RPG. You must have an active internet connection to play, as player progress and world states are handled server-side.

**Q: Why is there no sound in the tutorial after installation?**

A: This is a known issue related to incomplete resource downloads. Ensure that all optional audio packs have finished downloading in the background. If the issue persists, restarting the game or checking for a "Repair Files" option in the login menu usually fixes it.

**Q: Does the division resurgence file size differ between Android and iOS?**

A: The sizes are very similar, typically within 500MB of each other. However, iOS devices often require more temporary space during the actual installation process due to how the App Store handles file "unpacking."

**Q: Can I move the game to an SD card?**

A: While some Android devices allow moving app data to an SD card, it is strongly discouraged for The Division Resurgence. The slow read/write speeds of most SD cards will cause significant lag, texture popping, and potential crashes in the Dark Zone.

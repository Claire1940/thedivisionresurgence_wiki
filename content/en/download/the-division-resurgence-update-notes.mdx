## The Division 2 Year 8: Prototype Gear & Expertise

While Resurgence takes the spotlight for mobile users, the brand-wide update also introduces "Prototype Gear" to the ecosystem. This new rarity level pushes power creep further than ever before, allowing agents to enhance their current high-end and named items into superior versions. 

The **the division resurgence update notes** highlight that while this gear is rare, it is not gated behind impossible requirements. You do not need to be at the maximum expertise level to find Prototype drops in the new PvE Escalation Mode, though upgrading your existing gear does require a significant investment.

### Prototype Upgrade Requirements

| Requirement | Value/Level | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **Item Level** | 40 | Must be a max-level item |
| **Attributes** | Maxed | All core and secondary stats must be optimized |
| **Expertise Rank** | 30 | Required only for manual upgrades, not for looted drops |
| **Augments** | New Slot | Every prototype item gains a new passive "mini-talent" |

> **💡 Tip:** Focus on the "Escalation Mode" if you are under Expertise Level 30. This mode allows you to loot Prototype Gear directly without the heavy resource cost of manual upgrading.

## New Gameplay Modes: Realism and Escalation

The 2026 update introduces two major modes that change how agents approach the tactical landscape of New York and D.C. 

### Realism Mode
This 4-week anniversary season event introduces a high-stakes environment where tactical positioning is paramount. Realism Mode features a limited HUD, increased lethality for both players and enemies, and restricted respawns. It is designed for veteran players who want to experience the "survival" roots of the original Division.

### PvE Escalation
Escalation is a wave-based survival mode where the primary reward is the new Prototype Gear. As you progress through higher tiers, the difficulty spikes exponentially, but so does the chance of finding items with maxed-out "Augment" slots. 

1.  **Select Your Tier:** Higher tiers offer better loot but require a cohesive team.
2.  **Defend Objectives:** Waves alternate between area defense and high-value target elimination.
3.  **Extract Loot:** Unlike standard missions, you must successfully complete a "Boss Wave" to secure your Prototype rewards.

## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the official the division resurgence update notes?**

A: The full patch notes are typically shared during the official Ubisoft live streams on Twitch and YouTube, and subsequently posted on the official Ubisoft forums and the Resurgence website.

**Q: Is The Division Resurgence a separate game from The Division 2?**

A: Yes, Resurgence is a standalone mobile game with its own unique storyline set in New York. However, there are cross-promotional rewards that link your progress across both games via your Ubisoft Connect account.

**Q: What is the benefit of Prototype Gear?**

A: Prototype Gear offers higher base stats than High-End gear and includes a new "Augment" slot. These augments provide passive perks that can significantly boost your build's efficiency in high-level PvE content.

**Q: Can I play The Division Resurgence on my PC?**

A: While it is a mobile-first title, Resurgence supports various input methods and can be played on tablets or via official emulators/casting to a PC, provided you use a compatible controller or mouse/keyboard setup.

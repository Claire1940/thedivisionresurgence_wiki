## How to Change Language Settings in The Division Resurgence

Adjusting **the division resurgence mobile languages** is a straightforward process that can be done directly from the main menu or while in a safe zone. Because the game is a "live" environment, we recommend making these changes before heading out into the Dark Zone or high-intensity missions.

Follow these steps to update your preferences:

1.  **Launch the Game:** Open the app on your iOS or Android device.
2.  **Access Settings:** Tap the "Gear" icon located in the top-right corner of the HUD or the main menu screen.
3.  **Navigate to Language:** Locate the "Language" or "Localization" tab on the left-hand sidebar.

6.  **Restart if Prompted:** Some changes may require a quick restart of the application to apply the new text assets correctly.

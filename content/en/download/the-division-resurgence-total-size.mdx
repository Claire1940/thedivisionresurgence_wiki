## Exploring the Game Modes

The reason **the division resurgence total size** is so substantial lies in the variety of content available. Ubisoft has packed three distinct experiences into one application, ensuring that there is something for every type of player.

### 1. The Story Missions (Open World RPG)
The core of the game is the narrative campaign. Set between the events of *The Division 1* and *The Division 2*, you play as a SHD agent tasked with stabilizing Manhattan. This mode includes:
*   **Main Missions:** High-stakes operations that provide massive XP and narrative progression.
*   **Side Quests:** Faster missions scattered across the map, perfect for quick leveling.
*   **World Events:** Dynamic encounters like hostage rescues and weapon shipment interceptions.

### 2. The Dark Zone (PvPvE)
The Dark Zone remains the most intense way to play. It is a contaminated area where the rules of engagement are fluid. You can team up with strangers, but betrayal is always a possibility. The goal is to find high-tier loot and extract it via helicopter before the 20-minute timer expires.

### 3. PvP Domination
For those who prefer pure competitive action, Domination offers 4v4 matches on smaller, focused maps. The objective is to capture and hold three flags (A, B, and C). Because these maps are smaller, the action is significantly faster than the open-world segments.

## Understanding Your Enemies

The streets of New York are occupied by four major factions, each with distinct behaviors and threat levels. Knowing who you are fighting is just as important as having the right gear.

1.  **The Raiders:** Disorganized civilians turned to crime. They primarily use molotovs and basic firearms. Low threat.
2.  **The Rikers:** Escaped convicts from Rikers Island. They are aggressive and seek revenge against all authority. Moderate threat.
3.  **The Cleaners:** Former sanitation workers who want to burn the virus out of the city—literally. They use flamethrowers that deal massive burn damage. High threat.
4.  **The Freemen:** A highly organized group of scientists and engineers. They use advanced gadgets and turrets. Very high threat.

## Tips for New Agents

*   **Always Shoot Red Barrels:** Environmental hazards are your best friend. A single shot to a red barrel can wipe out a cluster of Rikers faster than a full magazine to the head.
*   **Compare Within Categories:** When checking weapon stats, only compare Snipers to Snipers or SMGs to SMGs. A Sniper will always have higher base damage, but that doesn't make it better for a close-quarters skirmish.
*   **Don't Skip Side Missions:** While main missions give the most XP, side missions are much faster. They are the most efficient way to bridge the level gap between major story beats.
*   **Trust No One in the Dark Zone:** If you aren't in a pre-made lobby with friends, assume every "ally" is waiting for the extraction timer to turn rogue and steal your loot.

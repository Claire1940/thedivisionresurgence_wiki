The Division Resurgence takes us back to Manhattan, but with a fresh perspective. You play as a member of the "First Wave" of SHD agents. The story bridges gaps between the first and second games, introducing new threats that require tactical coordination.

A significant addition to the lore in 2026 is the **Free Men**. This faction consists of engineers, construction workers, and blue-collar survivors who have turned their tools into weapons of war. Unlike the chaotic Rikers or the fanatical Cleaners, the Free Men are organized and utilize improvised heavy machinery and radio-controlled drones to defend their territory.

### Returning Threats
While the Free Men are the new kids on the block, classic enemies return with updated AI behaviors:

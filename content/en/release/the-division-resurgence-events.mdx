## How to Claim Your Event Rewards

While the official instructions suggest using the Ubisoft Connect rewards tab, many Agents have found that the process during **the division resurgence events** can be slightly more automated—or occasionally more temperamental—than described. 

### Official Claiming Method
1.  **Launch Ubisoft Connect:** This can be done via the mobile app, the PC client, or the in-game menu.
2.  **Navigate to Rewards:** Select the specific game (*Resurgence* or *Division 2*).
3.  **Unlock Items:** Choose the rewards you wish to claim.
4.  **Check Inventory:** The items should appear in your apparel or stash.

### The "Direct Login" Shortcut
Reports from the field suggest that for many players, the rewards are automatically injected into the account upon detection of gameplay. In *The Division 2*, you may not see the items in the Ubisoft Connect menu at all. Instead, check your **Apparel** menu directly. You should see the Resurgence hoodie, t-shirt, and hat added to your collection without manual intervention.

In *The Division Resurgence*, the process involves a few extra steps within the mobile interface:
1.  Open the **Main Menu** and click on the **Calendar** icon.
2.  Look for the **Rewards Claim** section.
3.  If "Online Rewards Available" is displayed, tap **Claim**.
4.  Travel to the **Base of Operations**.
5.  Access your **Stash** to pick up the actual packages containing your gear.

## Troubleshooting Common Event Issues

Participating in **the division resurgence events** isn't always seamless. Server heavy-loading during the 2026 launch window has led to delays in reward delivery. If you have played both games on the same account but haven't seen your items, consider the following troubleshooting steps.

*   **Account Sync:** Double-check that your mobile device is logged into the exact same Ubisoft Connect account as your console or PC. A common error is using a guest account on mobile.
*   **The "Hunter Mask" Bug:** In *The Division Resurgence*, some vanity items like the Washington DC hat may not appear if you have certain masks (like Hunter masks) equipped. Try unequipping all headgear to see if the item appears in your list.
*   **Server Propagation:** Rewards can take up to 24 hours to sync across the Ubisoft ecosystem. If you just finished your first session of *Resurgence*, wait a day before contacting support.
*   **Apparel Tab vs. Stash:** Remember that *The Division 2* rewards go directly to the Apparel tab, whereas *Resurgence* rewards often go to the Stash first as a "Package."

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not delete your character or reinstall the game if rewards are missing. This is usually a server-side entitlement issue that Ubisoft Support can resolve once the launch traffic stabilizes.

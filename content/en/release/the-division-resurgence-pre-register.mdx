## Understanding Game Modes: Missions, Dark Zone, and PvP

The game offers a diverse range of activities catering to different playstyles. For those who enjoy the narrative, the Main Missions provide the bulk of the story and the highest XP gains. Side Quests are shorter, faster alternatives perfect for quick sessions while commuting.

### The Dark Zone (PvPvE)
The Dark Zone remains the most intense experience in the game. In this walled-off section of Manhattan, you face both powerful NPCs and other real players. The objective is to scavenge high-tier loot and reach an extraction point. However, any player can go "Rogue" and betray you to steal your gear. It is generally recommended to enter the Dark Zone with a pre-made squad of friends rather than relying on random matchmaking to avoid betrayal.

### PvP Domination
For those who prefer pure competitive action, PvP Domination offers 4v4 matches on smaller, focused maps. Teams compete to capture and hold three objectives (A, B, and C). This mode is fast-paced and rewards players who understand map flow and team synergy.

## Enemy Factions and Tactical Combat

New York is divided among several hostile groups, each with distinct behaviors and weaknesses. Understanding who you are fighting allows you to adjust your tactics accordingly. For instance, shooting the fuel tanks on a Cleaner's back causes an explosion that deals massive damage to surrounding enemies.

*   **Raiders:** Desperate civilians using Molotovs; low threat but can flush you out of cover.
*   **Rikers:** Escaped convicts who are highly aggressive and use flanking maneuvers.
*   **Cleaners:** Fire-obsessed former workers; dangerous at close range due to flamethrowers.
*   **Freemen:** Highly organized scientists and engineers using advanced gadgets and technology.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never ignore the "Red Barrels" in the environment. While they are a staple of the genre, in this game, they deal significant environmental damage that can instantly clear a room of Raiders or Rikers.

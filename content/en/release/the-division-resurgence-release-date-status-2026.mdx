## Setting the Scene: Manhattan After the Fall

The narrative of Resurgence takes us back to the roots of the series. Players step into the boots of a first-wave SHD agent in Manhattan. The story unfolds shortly after the initial events of the first game, providing a fresh perspective on the chaos that followed the Green Poison outbreak. In addition to returning threats, players must contend with the **Free Men**, a new faction introduced specifically for this title. These are not just disorganized rioters; they are well-equipped and highly motivated individuals who believe the Division has failed the people. Their presence adds a layer of political complexity to the mission of reclaiming New York.

> **Warning:** Manhattan is a shared open world. While you can meet friendly agents spontaneously, always stay alert for hostile NPCs and environmental hazards that can end a solo run quickly.

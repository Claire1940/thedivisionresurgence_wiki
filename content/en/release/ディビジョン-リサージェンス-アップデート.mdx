## Faction Intel: Who You Are Fighting

Navigating the urban jungle requires a deep understanding of your enemies. The **ディビジョン リサージェンス アップデート** has tweaked the AI behavior of several key factions, making them more aggressive and coordinated. You will no longer find enemies simply standing in the open; they will use flanking maneuvers, suppressive fire, and specialized gadgets to flush you out of cover.

### The Rikers and The Cleaners
The Rikers, consisting of escaped convicts from Rikers Island, remain a chaotic threat, focusing on overwhelming numbers and raw firepower. Conversely, the Cleaners are more methodical, using flamethrowers and incendiary grenades to "sanitize" the city.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Cleaners have increased the range of their flamethrowers in the 2026 patch. Aim for their oxygen tanks to cause an explosion, but keep your distance to avoid the blast radius.

### The New Enemy Threat
A mysterious new paramilitary group has surfaced in the latest **ディビジョン リサージェンス アップデート**. Unlike the disorganized rioters, this group uses SHD-level technology, including seeker mines and automated turrets. Engaging them requires a high degree of tactical coordination and the use of EMP skills to neutralize their tech.

## Agent Specializations and Skill Customization

To combat these rising threats, the Division has authorized new skill variants. The **ディビジョン リサージェンス アップデート** allows for deeper customization of your signature weapons. The Dark Zone remains the most intense area of the game, and the **ディビジョン リサージェンス アップデート** has introduced "Contaminated Zones" that shift location every hour. These areas offer the highest quality loot but require specialized filters and constant vigilance against other players who may turn Rogue.

However, the tension remains high, as the 2026 update has increased the rewards for successfully extracting "Manhunt" caches.

> **💡 Tip:** When extracting in the Dark Zone, always place your turret near the extraction rope. It provides cover fire while you are vulnerable during the attachment animation.

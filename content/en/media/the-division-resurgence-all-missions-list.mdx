## Side Missions and Open World Activities

Side missions are shorter, more focused encounters scattered across the map. While they offer less XP than main missions, they are often much faster to complete, making them an excellent choice for filling the gap when you hit a progression blocker.

### Key Side Mission Types:
*   **Hostage Rescue:** Save civilians from hostile factions like the Raiders or Rikers.
*   **Supply Drop Recovery:** Secure SHD tech before enemy engineers can dismantle it.
*   **Arms Deal Interruption:** Prevent hostile groups from acquiring high-grade weaponry.
*   **Commendation Hunts:** Collect intel and field data to boost your SHADE level.

In addition to side quests, the open world is filled with repeatable activities. In 2026, agents are encouraged to complete at least 10 of these daily to maximize their resource gain. After the tenth completion, you will still earn loot, but the bonus XP is significantly reduced.

## Replaying Missions: Difficulty Tiers

One of the core features of the **the division resurgence all missions list** is the ability to replay content. Once a mission is cleared on "Story" mode, you can return to it at higher difficulties for better rewards.

*   **Normal:** The standard experience for leveling.
*   **Hard:** Enemies have more health and better aim. Elite (purple) enemies appear more frequently.
*   **Challenging:** Requires a coordinated group and a solid build. Named bosses feature unique mechanics.
*   **Heroic/Legendary:** The ultimate test for endgame agents. These missions often swap out standard factions for elite units with specialized gadgets.

## High-Stakes Endgame: The Dark Zone and Nests

Beyond the standard **the division resurgence all missions list**, there are specialized modes designed for high-level play. These areas provide the most valuable loot in the game but come with significant risks.

### The Dark Zone (DZ)
The Dark Zone is a 20-minute extraction-based session. Here, you face elite AI and other players. Any loot you find is "contaminated" and must be extracted via helicopter. If you die before extraction, you lose everything. In 2026, the Dark Zone also features "Targeted Loot," allowing you to hunt for specific gear pieces or weapon types.

### Challenging Nests
Nests are repeatable endgame activities that rotate every 24 hours. They are similar to "Strongholds" and provide a consistent source of High-End and Exotic materials.

### Lone Wolf Challenge
A solo-only mode that functions similarly to a "horde mode" or "climb the tower" system. It operates on a 21-day cycle, rewarding players who can clear stages the fastest. This mode is excellent for testing your build's survivability without relying on a squad.

## Rewards and Progression Tips

As you work through the **the division resurgence all missions list**, your focus should be on your **Power Score**. This score is an aggregate of your gear stats, weapon levels, and specialization points.

1.  **Dismantle, Don't Sell:** Early in the game, dismantling useless gear provides "Worn Metal Components" and "Worn Fabric," which are essential for the crafting bench.
2.  **Match Damage Types:** Pay attention to weapon icons. Red (Blast) is for groups, Light Blue (Shredding) destroys shields, and Dark Blue (Piercing) handles heavy armor.
3.  **Upgrade Consistently:** At level 20, you unlock the ability to recalibrate and improve gear. Ensure your primary weapon is always leveled up to match your character level.
4.  **Join a Clan:** Being part of a clan provides passive resource bonuses and access to clan-specific missions that aren't on the standard world map.

## FAQ

**Q: How many missions are in the main campaign?**

A: While the exact number grows with seasonal updates, there are currently 18 main campaign story missions and 15 primary side missions available in the core New York map for 2026.

**Q: Can I complete the division resurgence all missions list solo?**

A: Most story and side missions are designed to be solo-friendly. However, Challenging and Heroic difficulties, as well as certain Dark Zone landmarks, are significantly easier and more rewarding when played in a group.

**Q: What happens if I fail a mission?**

A: If you are defeated during a mission, you will typically respawn at the last checkpoint. In the Dark Zone, however, failing to extract means you lose all contaminated loot collected during that session.

**Q: Do missions scale with my level?**

A: Yes, the open-world activities and side quests scale to your current character level. Main missions have "recommended" Power Scores, but their difficulty can be adjusted when replaying them after the initial story completion.

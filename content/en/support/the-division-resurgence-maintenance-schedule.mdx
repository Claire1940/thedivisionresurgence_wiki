## Common Error Codes and Fixes

Post-maintenance, players sometimes encounter specific error codes. Knowing what these mean can save you time troubleshooting your own internet connection when the problem is actually server-side.

*   **Delta-03:** A general connection timeout. This often happens if your local game version doesn't match the server version. Check for an update in the App Store or Google Play.
*   **Mike-01:** This error indicates that the game cannot reach the account service. This is common if the maintenance has been extended.
*   **Bravo-04:** Usually related to a localized ISP issue or a VPN conflict. Disable your VPN and try a direct connection.

| Error Code | Primary Cause | Recommended Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Delta-01/03** | **Connectivity Loss** | Restart Router / Check for App Update |
| **Mike-01** | **Server Maintenance** | Wait for official "All Clear" on Twitter |
| **Echo-02** | **Account Data Error** | Log out and log back into Ubisoft Connect |
| **Sierra** | **Download Failure** | Ensure at least 15GB of free space on device |

## FAQ

**Q: How long does the division resurgence maintenance schedule usually last?**

A: Standard weekly maintenance typically lasts between 2 and 3 hours. However, major seasonal updates or patches introducing new story content can take up to 8 hours depending on the complexity of the backend changes.

**Q: Why can't I update the game after maintenance ends?**

A: Mobile storefronts like the Apple App Store and Google Play Store sometimes take longer to propagate the update to all regions. If you don't see an "Update" button, try clearing the cache of your store app or restarting your device to force a refresh.

**Q: Will I lose my progress if the server goes down while I'm in a mission?**

A: The Division Resurgence uses frequent server-side checkpoints. While you may lose progress within a specific combat encounter, your overall character experience, loot collected prior to the last save, and quest status are usually preserved. However, it is always best to avoid starting long incursions within 30 minutes of a scheduled shutdown.

**Q: Can I play The Division Resurgence offline during maintenance?**

A: No. The Division Resurgence is an "always-online" title. All character data, inventory, and world states are stored on Ubisoft's servers to prevent cheating and facilitate the shared-world experience. You must have an active connection to the servers to access any part of the game.

2.  **Gray Arrow:** Your daily "boost" has been depleted. You can still play and earn XP, but it will be at a significantly reduced rate.

## Troubleshooting Connectivity and Login Errors

If you are certain that **the division resurgence server status** is active but you still cannot log in, follow these standard troubleshooting steps for the 2026 mobile environment:

1.  **Check for Mandatory Updates:** Mobile titles often require small hotfixes that don't always trigger an automatic download. Check the App Store or Google Play Store manually.
2.  **Verify Power Score Requirements:** Some endgame content and campaign steps are locked behind a "Power Score." If a mission won't start, it isn't a server error; you likely need to upgrade your gear or mods to meet the minimum threshold.
3.  **Fast Travel Reset:** If an open-world activity isn't triggering, try fast traveling to a nearby safe house and back. This refreshes your instance on the server.
4.  **Dismantle Warning:** If you are unable to upgrade gear, ensure you have enough materials. Remember that dismantling gear only returns **90%** of invested materials (and only **80%** for OS Protocols).

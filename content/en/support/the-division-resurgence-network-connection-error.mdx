## Primary Fix: Switching Network Environments

The most common trigger for **the division resurgence network connection error** is an unstable "handshake" between your ISP and the game's regional servers. Surprisingly, a faster connection isn't always a more stable one for this specific title. 

### Transitioning from Wi-Fi to Mobile Data
Many players find that while their home Wi-Fi is excellent for streaming, its security protocols or DNS settings may conflict with the game's live service requirements. If you are on Wi-Fi, try the following:
1. Close the game completely and remove it from your "recent apps" list.
2. Toggle your Wi-Fi off and enable 5G or LTE mobile data.
3. Relaunch the game.
4. If the game loads successfully, you can often toggle Wi-Fi back on once you have reached the main character selection screen.

### Using a Mobile Hotspot
If your primary mobile data is also failing, connecting to a friend's hotspot (preferably on a different carrier) can bypass local routing issues. This helps determine if the problem lies with your specific service provider or the device itself.

## Device-Level Optimization and Cache Clearing

Sometimes, **the division resurgence network connection error** is caused by corrupted temporary files stored on your device. This is particularly common after a major patch or seasonal update in 2026.

### Clearing Cache on Android
1. Open your device **Settings**.
2. Navigate to **Apps** or **App Management**.
3. Locate **The Division Resurgence** in the list.
4. Tap on **Storage**.
5. Select **Clear Cache** (Do NOT select Clear Data unless you have backed up your account, as this will delete your login info).
6. Restart your phone and attempt to log in again.

### Refreshing Network Settings (iOS and Android)
A simple "Airplane Mode" toggle can often force your device to request a new IP address from your provider, which can clear up stagnant routing paths.
*   Enable **Airplane Mode** for 30 seconds.
*   Disable it and wait for your data signal to return to full strength.
*   Launch the game immediately.

## FAQ

**Q: Why am I getting error code 6000010 specifically during peak hours?**

A: Error 6000010 is often a timeout error. During peak hours, server congestion may prevent your device from receiving a response within the allotted timeframe. Try using a VPN to route through a less busy region or switch to a high-speed 5G connection.

**Q: Will clearing my cache delete my character progress?**

A: No. Clearing the cache only removes temporary files used to speed up loading. Your character data is stored on Ubisoft's servers. However, avoid selecting "Clear Data" or "Uninstall" unless you have linked your account to a Ubisoft Connect, Google, or Apple ID.

**Q: Can I fix the division resurgence network connection error by changing my DNS?**

A: Yes, many players find success by changing their device DNS to Google (8.8.8.8) or Cloudflare (1.1.1.1). This can help resolve issues where your ISP's default DNS fails to resolve the game's server addresses.

**Q: What should I do if the game is stuck at 99% on the program update?**

A: This is a specific type of **the division resurgence network connection error** related to file verification. First, check your storage space. If space is not an issue, switch from Wi-Fi to mobile data for the final 1% of the download, as mobile networks sometimes handle the small verification packets more effectively than filtered Wi-Fi.

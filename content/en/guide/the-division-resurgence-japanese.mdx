## The Rise of the Freemen Faction

One of the most dangerous additions to the Manhattan landscape is the Freemen. Unlike the chaotic Rikers or the fanatical Cleaners, the Freemen are led by a man of immense intellect and resources: Arthur King. Formerly a darling of Wall Street and Silicon Valley, King was a maverick inventor who cashed out of the limelight just before the pandemic struck.

King’s descent into villainy is a tragic tale of grief and paranoia. Convinced that the government was responsible for the Dollar Flu to strip citizens of their liberties, he retreated into the shadows with a dedicated group of followers. When his own wife and son succumbed to the disease despite his efforts, King’s distrust transformed into a bitter, focused hatred for the Division and any remaining government authority.

## Combat Strategies for 2026

The combat in *The Division Resurgence* is built on the foundation of tactical cover-based shooting. However, the introduction of the Freemen has forced agents to adapt. Follow these steps to maintain the upper hand:

*   **Utilize Verticality:** Many Manhattan streets offer accessible rooftops and scaffolding. Gaining the high ground is essential when facing the Freemen’s ground-based drones.
*   **Target Weak Points:** Every enemy archetype has a vulnerability. For Cleaners, it is their fuel tanks; for the Freemen, it is the battery packs on their specialized gear.
*   **Coordinate Skills:** In multiplayer sessions, ensure your team has a balance of offensive and defensive skills. A well-placed Restorer Hive can be the difference between a successful extraction and a wipe.

> **Tip:** Keep an eye out for "First Wave" caches scattered throughout the city. These contain rare gear that can significantly boost your power level early in the game.

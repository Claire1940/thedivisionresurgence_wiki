## FAQ

**Q: What is the single best weapon for solo players in The Division Resurgence?**

A: The **Warlord** (Exotic Assault Rifle) is generally considered the best for solo play due to its defensive talent that reduces incoming damage while firing, giving you much-needed survivability when outnumbered.

**Q: How do I reroll bad talents on my weapons?**

A: Use the **Recalibration Station** at the Base of Operations. You can spend Weapon Parts and Phoenix Credits to change one talent on a High-End weapon. Note that once you change one talent, the others become locked.

**Q: Is Gear Score more important than weapon talents?**

A: No. A lower Gear Score weapon with perfect talents (like Destructive and Ferocious) will almost always outperform a max Gear Score weapon with useless talents. You can eventually use the **Optimization Station** to bring your "God Roll" weapon up to max level.

**Q: Where is the best place to find the "ディビジョンリサージェンス最強" Exotic SMG, Lady Death?**

A: Lady Death is most commonly found as a drop from Named Bosses in the Dark Zone or purchased from the Dark Zone Vendor using DZ Credits. It occasionally appears in the Exotic Cache rewards from Weekly Projects.

**Q: Do I need to wait 24 hours for crafting?**

A: While the crafting bench often imposes a real-time timer for high-end blueprints, it is usually more efficient to farm Nests or Resistance for drops rather than relying solely on the workbench for your primary gear.

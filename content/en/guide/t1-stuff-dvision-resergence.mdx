## Initial Setup: Servers and Performance

Your journey begins before you even fire your first bullet. One of the most significant choices you will make is your server region. The Division Resurgence features three primary servers: American, European, and Asian. While the game supports full crossplay between iOS, Android, and PC emulators, these servers are entirely isolated.

If you plan to play with friends or join a specific clan, you must all ensure you are on the same server. You cannot group with players on the European server if your character is on the American server. Fortunately, the netcode is robust; many players find that playing on a non-native region results in minimal input lag, making it possible to prioritize social connections over geographic proximity.

> **💡 Tech Tip:** By default, the game may run at 30fps. To ensure a competitive experience, navigate to your settings and enable the 45fps or 60fps options. This may lower your graphical fidelity slightly, but the increased responsiveness is vital for high-stakes combat.

## XP Efficiency and Daily Progression Caps

The Division Resurgence utilizes a daily XP boost system to prevent burnout and maintain a level playing field. Near your character level, look for an orange arrow. When this arrow is lit, you are receiving 100% XP gains. Once you have progressed through a certain amount of content, the arrow will turn gray, signaling that you will gain XP at a significantly reduced rate until the next daily reset.

The Dark Zone remains the most efficient place to farm **t1 stuff dvision resergence** loot and high-value XP, but you should also prioritize the following activities:

1.  **Daily Quests:** These provide massive chunks of XP and allow for five free rerolls per day.
2.  **Main Campaign:** The primary source of progression and story-based unlocks.
3.  **Nests:** Instanced open-world challenges. Look for "Heroic" nests, which stay active for 24 hours and offer superior rewards.
4.  **Open World Activities:** You can receive full rewards for up to 10 activities per day. Use fast travel directly to these icons to save time.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Side missions do not scale with your level in Resurgence. If you pick up a side mission at level 10, it will always provide level 10 XP. While they are still worth doing, do not feel pressured to "save" them for later like in The Division 2.

## Crafting and Dismantling Strategy

Crafting in Resurgence is a marathon, not a sprint. Unlike the instant crafting found in the console versions, Resurgence implements a 24-hour timer for most items. Furthermore, the quality of your crafted gear is heavily dependent on your workbench level, which requires a significant grind to upgrade.

> **💡 Tip:** It is generally recommended to avoid heavy investment in crafting during the early game. Instead, rely on loot drops and mission rewards. Save your materials for when you reach the level cap and can target specific endgame blueprints.

When you find yourself with a backpack full of unneeded gear, dismantling is almost always better than selling. Materials are the primary bottleneck for leveling up your existing gear. However, be aware that dismantling an item only returns 90% of the materials you previously invested in it. For OS Protocols, this return rate drops to 80%.

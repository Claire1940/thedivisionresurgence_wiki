## FAQ

**Q: Why is Division Resurgence not loading after the latest update?**

A: This is usually due to a "version mismatch" or corrupted update files. Try clearing your app cache or checking the App Store/Play Store to ensure the update fully completed. If the issue persists, the servers may be undergoing maintenance to stabilize the new patch.

**Q: Can a slow SD card cause loading issues?**

A: Yes. If you have moved the game to an external SD card, the read/write speeds may be too slow for the game's assets. For the best experience, always install Division Resurgence on your device's internal "UFS" storage.

**Q: Is there a way to check if the servers are down?**

A: Ubisoft typically posts server status updates on their official social media channels and the Ubisoft Support website. If many players are reporting **division resurgence not loading** at the same time, it is almost certainly a server-side issue that requires patience rather than troubleshooting.

**Q: Does using a VPN get you banned?**

A: Generally, no. Many players use VPNs to improve routing. However, using a VPN to bypass regional pricing or to gain an unfair advantage in matchmaking can violate the Terms of Service. For troubleshooting loading issues, a VPN is a safe and common tool.

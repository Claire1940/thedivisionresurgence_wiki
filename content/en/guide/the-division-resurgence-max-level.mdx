## End-Game Gear: Mods and Recalibration

After hitting the **the division resurgence max level**, your power is largely dictated by your gear's attributes and mods. Targeted loot farming is a key feature in 2026. By checking your map, you can identify which districts or missions have a higher drop rate for specific gear slots or weapon types.

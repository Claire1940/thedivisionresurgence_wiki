## Standard Weapon Categories and Playstyles

Beyond the rare exotics, your loadout will primarily consist of standard weapon classes. It is vital to compare weapons within the same category. Comparing a Sniper Rifle's damage to an SMG's damage is misleading because their roles on the battlefield are entirely different.

### Assault Rifles (AR)
The most versatile choice for any mission. ARs like the **P416** or **AK-M** provide a balance of range and fire rate. They are the go-to for mid-range encounters against Rikers and Raiders.

### Submachine Guns (SMG)
Designed for close-quarters combat (CQC). SMGs have high burst damage and better critical hit chances. They are highly effective in the tight corridors of the New York subway system.

### Marksman Rifles (MMR)
For players who prefer to stay back. MMRs offer the highest per-bullet damage. Aiming for headshots is essential, as the fire rate is significantly lower than other classes.

### Shotguns
The ultimate "stop-gap" weapon. Shotguns are perfect for dealing with aggressive "Cleaner" rushers who try to flank your position.

## Crafting and Upgrading Your Arsenal

You will often find weapons during missions that have lower stats than your current gear. Do not leave these behind! The crafting system is a pillar of progression in 2026.

1.  **Dismantle for Materials:** Breaking down unwanted gear provides credits and components.
2.  **Visit the Crafting Station:** Use collected materials to build specific weapon types or upgrade your existing "OS Protocols."
3.  **Weapon Mods:** Enhance your favorite firearms with scopes, extended magazines, and grips to improve stability and accuracy.
4.  **Recalibration:** If a weapon has one bad stat but high firepower, use the recalibration bench to swap out the undesirable trait.

## Enemy Factions: Who are you shooting at?

Your choice from the **the division resurgence 武器一覧** should also depend on who you are fighting. Different factions require different tactical approaches.

*   **Raiders:** Low-threat civilians turned criminals. They mostly use Molotovs and basic pistols.
*   **Rikers:** Escaped convicts who are highly aggressive. High-capacity magazines are recommended to deal with their numbers.
*   **Cleaners:** Former workers who use flamethrowers to "sanitize" the city. Keep your distance with ARs or Rifles to avoid their fire damage.
*   **Freemen:** The most dangerous group. These are scientists and engineers led by Arthur King. They use gadgets, turrets, and advanced tactics, requiring you to use your Specialization skills effectively.

## Best Weapons for Different Game Modes

The Division Resurgence offers three distinct ways to play, and your loadout should reflect the challenges of each.

### Story Missions & Side Quests
Focus on sustain and versatile damage. An Assault Rifle paired with a Shotgun or SMG allows you to handle both open-street fights and indoor room clearing.

### The Dark Zone (PvPvE)
In the Dark Zone, you face both NPCs and other players. High burst damage is king here. Weapons like the **Lady Death** or high-end Shotguns are preferred because encounters with rogue agents happen fast and at close range.

### PvP Domination
This is a 4v4 mode focused on capturing flags (A, B, and C). Since the maps are smaller and more action-packed, high-stability weapons and AoE skills (like the Demolitionist's turret) are highly effective for holding capture points.

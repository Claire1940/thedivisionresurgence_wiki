## Step-by-Step: How to Claim Your Rewards

The official method for claiming these rewards involves the [Ubisoft Connect](https://ubisoftconnect.com/) platform. While some players have reported items appearing automatically, following the manual claim process is the most reliable way to ensure you don't miss out.

### Claiming in The Division 2 (PC/Console)

1.  **Launch The Division 2**: Open the game on your preferred platform.
2.  **Access the Menu**: Open the main character menu and navigate to the "Ubisoft Connect" tab.
3.  **Navigate to Rewards**: Once the Ubisoft Connect overlay opens, select the "Rewards" section.
4.  **Redeem Items**: Look for the Resurgence-themed items. They should be listed as "Free" or "Unlocked" based on your mobile play session.
5.  **Check Apparel**: After claiming, go to your in-game Apparel menu. The items should be added directly to your inventory.

### Claiming in The Division Resurgence (Mobile)

1.  **Open the Game**: Launch *The Division Resurgence* on your iOS or Android device.
2.  **Open the Calendar**: On the main UI (top menu), look for the Calendar icon.
3.  **Rewards Claim**: Tap on the "Rewards Claim" tab. You should see a notification stating "Online rewards available."
4.  **Claim from Stash**: After claiming them in the menu, you must visit the Base of Operations. Go to your **Stash** and look for the "Inbox" or "Packages" section to move the items into your active inventory.

## Troubleshooting Missing Division 2 Resurgence Rewards

Despite the official instructions, the launch window in 2026 has seen some technical hiccups. Many players have reported that the **division 2 resurgence rewards** do not appear in the Ubisoft Connect "Rewards" tab even after fulfilling the requirements.

### Common Issues and Solutions

*   **The "Auto-Inject" Phenomenon**: Some players find that they do not need to claim the items at all. If you log into *The Division 2* and see a notification in the bottom-left corner saying "Received Resurgence Hat/Hoodie," the items have been automatically added to your apparel. Always check your apparel inventory before assuming the system is broken.
*   **Server Sync Delays**: Due to the high volume of players following the March 31st launch, there can be a delay of up to 24-48 hours between playing one game and the rewards triggering in the other.
*   **The "Hunter Mask" Bug**: In *The Division Resurgence*, some players have noted that the Washington DC cap doesn't appear if they have a full-face mask (like a Hunter mask) equipped. Try removing your headwear to see if the reward is actually there.
*   **Stash vs. Inventory**: Remember that in the mobile version, rewards do not go straight to your backpack. You must manually retrieve them from the Stash at the Base of Operations.

> **💡 Tip:** If the rewards still don't show up after 48 hours, try logging into the Ubisoft Connect website on a PC browser. Sometimes the web interface forces a refresh that the in-game overlay misses.

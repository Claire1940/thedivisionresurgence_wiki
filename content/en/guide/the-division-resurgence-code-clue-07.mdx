These combinations are never randomized; they are baked into the game world. You can find them on:

## Base of Operations Hidden Safes

The Base of Operations (BoO) is more than just a social hub; it contains several safes that beginners often overlook. These are excellent for getting a head start on your build before venturing into the Dark Zone.

### The Dormitory Safe
Located in the residential wing where agents spawn after fast traveling. Look for the notice board before entering the sleeping quarters. A small slip of paper contains the code **1350**. This safe is crucial because it often drops blueprints for early-game weapon mods.

### The Madison Street Loop
Directly outside the BoO, if you take a left toward the Madison Field Hospital (a callback to the original game), you will find a series of storefronts.
*   **The Serotentia Safe:** This is a large floor safe. To open it, you must first pick up the "Birthday Gift" collectible on the ground outside the tent. The clue mentions November 5th, making the code **1105**.
*   **The Supermarket:** Located across from the Oaklock store. The code **1987** is visible on a large red sign above the frozen food section.

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep your "Scavenging" skill upgraded in your specialization tree to increase the quality of loot dropped from these world safes.

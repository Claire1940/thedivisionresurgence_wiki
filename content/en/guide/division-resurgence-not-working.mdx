## Initial Troubleshooting and Quick Fixes

The first and most effective step is a full device restart. This clears the system RAM and terminates any background processes that might be competing with the game for resources. Once your device has rebooted, attempt to launch the game again to see if the stability has improved.

| Action | Purpose | Expected Result |
|---|---|---|

## Managing App Data and Cache

Over time, the cached files stored by the game can become bloated or corrupted, leading to the **division resurgence not working** error. Clearing the cache is a safe way to refresh the app without losing your login credentials or downloaded assets. However, if the issues persist, a "Clear Data" operation may be necessary.

> **Warning:** Clearing app data will delete your local settings and may require you to re-download significant portions of the game's assets. Ensure your account is linked to a Ubisoft Connect or social media profile before proceeding.

### How to Clear Cache and Data on Android:
1. Navigate to your device **Settings**.
2. Tap on **Apps** or **App Management**.
3. Locate **The Division Resurgence** in the list.
4. Select **Storage Usage**.
5. Tap **Clear Cache**. If the problem persists, return here and select **Clear Data**.

## FAQ

**Q: Why does Division Resurgence keep crashing on the loading screen?**

A: This is often caused by insufficient RAM or corrupted cache files. Try closing all background applications and clearing the game's cache in your device settings. If the device is older, lowering the graphics settings immediately upon entry can also prevent crashes.

**Q: Is "Division Resurgence not working" a sign that my phone is unsupported?**

A: It is possible. The Division Resurgence requires a high-end processor and at least 4GB of RAM (6GB+ recommended). Check the minimum requirements on the app store page; if your device falls below these specs, the game may fail to start or crash frequently.

**Q: I updated the game, but now it won't open at all. What should I do?**

A: Update-related failures are common if the installation was interrupted. The best course of action is to uninstall the game, restart your phone, and perform a clean reinstallation to ensure all new files are correctly indexed.

**Q: How can I tell if the Division Resurgence servers are down?**

A: Check the official "The Division Resurgence" Twitter/X account or the Ubisoft Support website. Community hubs like Reddit or Discord are also excellent places to see if other players are reporting similar "Not Working" issues in real-time.

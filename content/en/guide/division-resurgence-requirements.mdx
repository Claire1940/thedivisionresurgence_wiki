## Gear Tier Requirements: The Tier 2 Hunt

In The Division Resurgence, gear color (Purple for Superior, Gold for High-End) is not the only metric for power. The introduction of **Gear Tiers** adds a new layer to the **division resurgence requirements** for competitive builds. While Tier 0 and Tier 1 items are common during the leveling process, Tier 2 gear is the gold standard for endgame performance.

Tier 2 gear provides significantly higher base stats compared to lower tiers. However, a well-rolled Tier 1 item with a Tier 2 talent can still be viable. The goal for any serious agent in 2026 is a full set of Tier 2 items that synergize with their chosen specialization.

### Where to Find Tier 2 Gear

| Source | Reliability | Difficulty | Matchmaking |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Elite Mission Challenge** | **High** | Legendary | Group Finder Only |
| **Dark Zone Heroic Bosses** | Low | Very High | Open/PvP |
| **Weekly Bonus Caches** | Guaranteed | Varies | First 2 completions |

The Elite Mission Challenge currently features eight main missions available on Legendary difficulty. It is important to note that this mode **does not support standard matchmaking**. You must use the in-game group finder or enter with a pre-made squad, making social coordination one of the hidden **division resurgence requirements** for high-level play.

## Skill Optimization: Chipsets and Targeted Loot

Skills in Resurgence are dictated by your specialization, but their effectiveness is determined by **Chipsets** (Skill Mods). Unlike previous Division titles, Chipsets in Resurgence feature set bonuses. Equipping multiple chips from the same set can provide massive boosts to skill haste, damage, or duration.

The Dark Zone is the primary source for these items. Fortunately, Ubisoft has implemented a "Targeted Loot" system for the DZ. Before entering, you can select which specialization focus you want to farm for. While this doesn't guarantee a specific drop, it significantly skews the RNG in your favor.

> **Warning:** While farming the Dark Zone, remember that loot is not yours until it is extracted or smuggled out. You can visit safe rooms to have an NPC smuggle items for a fee of DZ credits, bypassing the risky extraction process.

## OS Protocols: The Ultimate Build Enablers

Perhaps the most distinct of the **division resurgence requirements** for a "God-tier" build is the OS Protocol. These function as class-defining talents that can completely change how a specialization plays. OS Protocols come in various rarities, but the Gold (High-End) versions are notoriously difficult to obtain.

### OS Protocol Sources in 2026

1.  **Weekly Bounties:** This is the primary gameplay method. By defeating the main bounty target each week, you have a chance at Purple or Gold protocols.
2.  **Sealed Caches (Store):** These function as a gacha-style system. While you can use "Shade Keys" earned through gameplay, the drop rates for Gold protocols are extremely low.
3.  **Leveling Protocols:** To improve an OS Protocol, you must donate duplicate protocols of the same type.

Because of the duplicate requirement, a fully leveled Blue or Purple OS Protocol is often more powerful than a base-level Gold protocol. Do not feel pressured to spend money on caches; consistent participation in the weekly bounty system is the most sustainable way to meet your build's **division resurgence requirements**.

## FAQ

**Q: What are the minimum division resurgence requirements for Android devices?**

A: At a minimum, you need a device with Android 10, a Snapdragon 720G processor (or equivalent), and at least 4GB of RAM. However, for a smooth experience in 2026, 8GB of RAM is highly recommended.

**Q: Can I play the Elite Mission Challenge solo?**

A: While technically possible for the most elite players, these missions are designed for four-player groups. Since there is no matchmaking for Legendary difficulty, you must use the group finder or bring friends to meet the tactical **division resurgence requirements** for the mission.

**Q: Are Gold OS Protocols required for the endgame?**

A: No. While Gold protocols are powerful, they are very difficult to level up. A fully upgraded Purple or even Blue OS Protocol can outperform a base Gold protocol because of the stat bonuses gained from donating duplicates.

**Q: How do I get Tier 2 gear if I can't beat Legendary missions yet?**

A: You can occasionally find Tier 2 gear from Heroic roaming bosses and landmarks in the Dark Zone. It is a rarer drop than in Legendary missions, but it allows solo players a chance to gear up slowly.

## Android System Requirements

Android users face the widest variety of hardware configurations, making it essential to identify where your specific chipset falls within the performance tiers. Ubisoft has optimized the game to run on a range of processors, but for a "true AAA experience," higher-end silicon is preferred.

### Minimum Android Specifications
To simply get the game running in a playable state (typically 30fps at Low to Medium settings), your device must meet the following baseline. Devices below these specs may experience frequent crashes or severe graphical artifacts.

| Component | Minimum Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Processor (CPU)** | Snapdragon 700 Series / Snapdragon 855 |
| **RAM** | **4 GB** |
| **Operating System** | Android 10 or higher |
| **Graphics API** | Vulkan Support Recommended |

### Recommended Android Specifications
For those seeking high-fidelity textures, improved draw distances, and a stable 60fps, the recommended requirements are significantly steeper. This is especially important for competitive play in the Dark Zone where every frame counts.

| Component | Recommended Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Processor (CPU)** | Snapdragon 8 Gen 1 / 8 Gen 2 Plus or equivalent |
| **RAM** | **6 GB to 8 GB** |
| **Storage Space** | **15 GB** of high-speed internal storage |
| **OS Version** | Android 12 or newer |

> **Warning:** Running the game on the absolute minimum specs may cause your device to heat up rapidly. It is recommended to use a mobile cooling fan if you plan on playing for sessions longer than 30 minutes.

## iOS System Requirements

Apple users benefit from a more streamlined optimization process, but the graphical demands of Unreal Engine 4 still mean that older iPhones will struggle. The game requires a relatively modern architecture to handle the real-time lighting and physics of the New York streets.

### Minimum and Recommended iOS Hardware
The cutoff for iOS support is quite firm. If your device is older than the iPhone 11, you will likely be unable to download the game from the App Store.

| Feature | Minimum Requirement | Recommended for 60FPS |
|---|---|---|
| **Device Model** | **iPhone 11** or newer | **iPhone 13 / 14 / 15 Pro** |
| **OS Version** | iOS 13.0 or higher | Latest iOS 17+ |
| **RAM** | 4 GB (Standard on iPhone 11) | 6 GB+ (Pro Models) |
| **Tablet Support** | iPad with A12 Bionic or better | iPad Pro (M1/M2 Chips) |

For iPad players, the extra screen real estate allows for a much better tactical view, but the resolution scaling can be taxing. If you are using a base-model iPad, ensure you have adjusted the "Shadow Quality" and "Particle Effects" in the settings menu to maintain performance.

## Storage and Installation Size

One of the most overlooked aspects of **the division resurgence system requirements** is the actual footprint the game leaves on your storage. Unlike smaller mobile titles, this is a full-scale RPG with high-resolution assets.

1.  **Initial Download:** The initial installer from the Play Store or App Store is relatively small (approx. 2-3 GB).
2.  **In-Game Data Patch:** Upon first launch, the game will download an additional 8-12 GB of high-resolution assets.
3.  **Total Footprint:** You should have at least **15 GB of free space** available to account for the installation process and future updates.

> **Tip:** Always install The Division Resurgence on your device's internal storage rather than an external SD card. Internal storage offers significantly faster read/write speeds, which are necessary to prevent "hiccups" when loading new city blocks.

## Performance Optimization for 2026

Meeting the hardware requirements is only half the battle. To ensure the best experience on your specific device, consider the following optimization steps:

### Adjusting Graphical Presets
The game offers several presets: Low, Medium, High, and Ultra. If you notice your device getting hot, dropping from Ultra to High can significantly reduce thermal throttling without a massive loss in visual clarity.

### Frame Rate vs. Battery Life
You can toggle between 30fps and 60fps. While 60fps is smoother, it consumes battery roughly 40% faster. For long grinding sessions in the open world, 30fps is often more sustainable. For PvP encounters, always switch to 60fps.

### Network Stability
As an always-online game, your "system requirements" also include your internet connection. A ping of under 50ms is ideal for the Dark Zone. If you are playing on mobile data, ensure you have a 5G connection, as 4G LTE may struggle with the data throughput required for the game's synchronized world events.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I run The Division Resurgence on a 4GB RAM Android phone?**

A: Yes, 4GB of RAM is the minimum requirement. However, you should close all other background applications to ensure the game has access to the maximum available memory, otherwise, you may experience crashes during heavy combat.

**Q: Why does the game take up more than 10GB of space?**

A: The Division Resurgence features a massive open world with high-quality textures and audio files. To maintain the AAA experience, the developers use high-resolution assets that require significant storage. Total space needed is typically between 10GB and 15GB.

**Q: Is an internet connection required to play?**

A: Yes, the game is a persistent online world. You will need a stable Wi-Fi or 5G/LTE connection to play, even when completing solo missions.

**Q: What are the division resurgence system requirements for 60fps on iOS?**

A: To achieve a stable 60fps on iOS, an iPhone 13 or newer is highly recommended. While the iPhone 11 and 12 can run the game, they may struggle to maintain 60fps at higher graphical settings due to thermal limitations.

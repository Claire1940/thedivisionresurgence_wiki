## Detailed Safe Locations and Combinations

To help you track these down, we have broken down the specific paths to each safe. Efficiency is vital when farming for gear, so use the fast travel points listed below to save time.

### Spring Blossom Settlement Safe
This is likely the first safe most agents will encounter. After fast traveling to the Spring Blossom Settlement, head toward the upper levels of the residential area. Follow the stairs to the top floor until you enter a small room containing the safe.
*   **The Code:** 3580
*   **Where to find the clue:** Look at the chalkboard immediately to the right of the safe. The numbers are written clearly in white chalk.

### NYC Base of Operations: The Dormitories
Inside your main hub, there is a safe hidden within the living quarters. Enter the dormitories and look for the central notice board where agents post flyers.
*   **The Code:** 1350
*   **Where to find the clue:** A small piece of paper pinned to the notice board contains the four-digit sequence.

### Oaklock Store and Supermarket
These two safes are located very close to one another, just down the street from the main entrance of the Base of Operations. Take a right from the entrance and run down the block.
1.  **Oaklock Store:** Located on the left side of the street. The safe is on the floor. Use code **122**, which is found on the store's signage.
2.  **Supermarket:** Directly across the street from Oaklock. The safe is along the back wall. Use code **1987**, which refers to the year the store was "established," found on the wall above the safe.

### The Serotentia "Birthday Gift" Safe
This safe is unique because it requires a collectible to solve. It is located near the Madison Field Hospital site. Look for a collectible on the ground outside the Serotentia entrance labeled "Code Clue: Birthday Gift."
*   **The Code:** 1105
*   **The Logic:** The clue mentions a birthday on the 5th of November (11/05). Entering this into the large floor safe will unlock the reward.

## Safe House Codes: Autumn's Hope and Dante's Run

As you unlock new safe houses across the map, you will find that many of them serve as mini-hubs with their own hidden secrets. These are often the easiest **division resurgence codes** to miss because agents tend to fast travel and immediately leave the area.

### Autumn's Hope
Inside the Autumn's Hope safe house, head to the reception desk. The safe is tucked away near the NPC Mullins. 
*   **The Code:** 1883
*   **The Clue:** Look at the "Established" year on the sign behind the desk. Even if your graphics settings make it blurry, 1883 is the confirmed combination.

### Dante's Run
Exit the Dante's Run safe house through the main door and take a right toward the clothing store area (look for the mannequins). The safe is located behind the checkout counter.
*   **The Code:** 7716
*   **The Clue:** A bright poster on the front of the desk displays the numbers clearly.

### Kerman Station
At Kerman Station, exit the main spawn room and look for the "Safia Nerdies" lockers. 
*   **The Code:** 7069
*   **The Clue:** A small note is pinned directly to the side of the locker bank containing the safe.

## Troubleshooting and Tips for Finding Codes

If you encounter a safe not listed here, don't worry. The game features 11 "Cryptic Clues" in the collectibles menu that point toward additional hidden caches. To find them on your own, keep these tips in mind:

1.  **Check the Environment:** Most codes are within 10 virtual meters of the safe. Look at posters, chalkboards, and neon signs.
2.  **Collect Everything:** Some safes won't allow interaction until you have picked up the corresponding "clue" collectible in the nearby area.
3.  **Listen to your ISAC:** Your onboard AI will often announce "Object of Interest Detected" when you are near a locked safe or a clue.
4.  **Brightness Settings:** Some codes are written in dark ink on dark surfaces. If you're struggling to see a sign, momentarily increase your in-game brightness.

> **Warning:** Be careful when entering codes in "Contaminated Zones." Your filters have a limited duration, and spending too much time trying to guess a code can lead to a quick death. Always have the code ready before you enter.

## FAQ

**Q: Are division resurgence codes the same for every player?**

A: Yes, the safe combinations are static environmental puzzles. They do not change between accounts or sessions, meaning you can use the same codes listed in this guide to unlock rewards on any character.

**Q: Do these safes respawn after I open them?**

A: Generally, these specific locked safes are one-time rewards per character. However, they provide a significant boost to your resources and often contain "Superior" (purple) tier items that are very helpful for leveling.

**Q: Can I find promo codes for free items in Division Resurgence?**

A: While this guide focuses on in-game safe codes, Ubisoft occasionally releases redeemable promo codes during seasonal events or via social media. These are typically entered in the "Settings" or "Store" menu under a "Redeem Code" tab.

**Q: What should I do if a code doesn't work?**

A: First, ensure you are at the correct safe location. Some safes look identical but belong to different districts. Second, check if you have picked up any nearby collectibles, as some safes require the "Clue" to be in your inventory before the keypad becomes active.

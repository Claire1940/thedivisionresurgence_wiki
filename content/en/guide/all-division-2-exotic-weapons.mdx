## How to Get All Division 2 Exotic Weapons

Acquiring **all division 2 exotic weapons** requires a mix of luck, persistence, and strategic participation in specific game modes. The 2026 roadmap has introduced "Priority Objectives," a modernized project system that provides guaranteed exotic rewards for completing daily tasks.

### 1. The General Loot Pool (Targeted Farming)
Most exotics, such as the Chameleon or the Lady Death, can be found by simply playing the game with the correct targeted loot set.
*   **Countdown:** Run this on Challenging difficulty. It provides the highest loot-per-hour ratio and earns you Countdown Credits to buy Exotic Caches from the vendor.
*   **The Summit:** Useful for solo players who want to grind specific floors or complete the weekly 15-floor challenge for a guaranteed cache.

### 2. Activity-Locked Exotics
Several of the most powerful weapons cannot be found in general caches until you have earned them from their respective "home" activities.

| **The Bighorn** | Legendary Strongholds | 5% Drop from Legendary Bosses |

### 3. Exotic Quests and Manhunts
Certain weapons require you to follow a specific questline or complete seasonal Climax Missions.
*   **The Regulus:** Requires the Iron Horse Raid project completion.
*   **The Nemesis:** Involves collecting parts from Invaded Stronghold bosses over several weeks.
*   **The Capacitor:** Earned by completing 5 challenges in a single run of The Summit.
*   **Seasonal Manhunts:** Weapons like the *Tempest*, *Sheriff*, and *Whiplash* are guaranteed drops upon completing their respective Season Climax missions on any difficulty (Master difficulty guarantees it on the first clear).

## Meta Tier List: Best Exotic Weapons 2026

While collecting **all division 2 exotic weapons** is the goal, knowing which ones to prioritize for your build will save you countless hours in the Dark Zone and Legendary missions.

### S-Tier: The Game Changers
*   **St. Elmo's Engine (AR):** Remains the king of versatility. Its ability to generate shock ammo provides unparalleled crowd control for both solo and group play.
*   **Ouroboros (SMG):** With a blistering fire rate, it melts bosses in seconds. It is the mandatory choice for "Paradise Lost" speedruns.
*   **Scorpio (Shotgun):** The ultimate support weapon. It applies stacks of poison, disorientation, and shock, making it essential for stopping high-threat targets like Hunters or Rogue Agents.
*   **Regulus (Pistol):** For those who can land headshots, the explosive AOE damage makes it faster for clearing rooms than most primary weapons.

### A-Tier: Specialized Powerhouse
*   **Pestilence (LMG):** Excellent for "set and forget" damage. The DoT ticks can clear entire waves of enemies behind cover.
*   **Shroud (Marksman Rifle):** The newest favorite for snipers, offering massive crit potential and utility in Year 8 content.
*   **Capacitor (AR):** The gold standard for Skill Builds. It grants weapon damage based on your skill tiers and increases skill damage as you shoot.

## Optimizing Your Collection: Expertise and Rerolling

Once you have acquired your desired items from the list of **all division 2 exotic weapons**, the journey isn't over. You must optimize them to compete in 2026's "Master" difficulty tiers.

1.  **Reconfiguring (Rerolling):** Use the Crafting Bench to reroll the stats on your exotics. Note that exotic armor always has the same attributes, but **exotic weapons can have their 3rd attribute changed**. For example, you can reroll an AR to have "Damage to Target Out of Cover" for maximum DPS.
2.  **Expertise Level 30:** By becoming proficient with a weapon (using it or donating duplicates), you can upgrade its base damage. In 2026, the cap is Level 30, providing a massive boost to your total power.
3.  **Exotic Components:** Every duplicate exotic you find should be deconstructed. These components are the "gold" of the endgame, used to fuel Expertise upgrades and stat rerolls.

> **⚠️ Warning:** When reconfiguring an exotic, the original item is destroyed and replaced with a new version. If you already have a 90% "God Roll," it is often better to use the **Optimization Station** rather than gambling on a reroll.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I get the Eagle Bearer or Ouroboros from an Exotic Cache?**

A: No. These weapons are excluded from the general exotic cache pool. You must earn them through their specific activities (Dark Hours Raid and Paradise Lost Incursion) or potentially through the new Retaliation mode blueprints.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get exotic components in 2026?**

A: The fastest method is completing "Priority Objectives" and the weekly SHD Requisition project. Additionally, running Countdown on Challenging and purchasing Exotic Caches with credits provides a steady stream of items to deconstruct.

**Q: How many exotic weapons can I equip at once?**

A: You can only equip **one** exotic weapon and **one** exotic armor piece at any given time. This limitation makes choosing the right weapon for your specific build synergy crucial.

**Q: Is it possible to get all division 2 exotic weapons as a solo player?**

A: Most can be obtained solo via Countdown (matchmade), The Summit, and Open World farming. However, the Raid-exclusive weapons and the Ouroboros SMG generally require a coordinated group, though matchmaking for Retaliation mode in 2026 has made the blueprints more accessible to everyone.

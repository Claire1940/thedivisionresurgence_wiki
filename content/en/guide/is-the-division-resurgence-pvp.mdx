The "Rogue" mechanic is the heart of the Dark Zone. Players can choose to "go Rogue" at any time, allowing them to attack other SHD Agents to steal their unextracted loot. This creates a high-tension atmosphere where every encounter with another player is a potential threat.

| Rogue Status | Description | Risk Level |

## Tactical Strategies for Winning Matches
Winning in **is the division resurgence pvp** modes requires more than just fast reflexes. Because the game utilizes a cover-based system, positioning is the most critical factor in any engagement.

1.  **The "L" Flank:** While two teammates suppress the enemy from the front, one or two players should move wide to hit the enemy from the side.
2.  **Point Cycling:** In Domination, if the enemy team is heavily defending Point Bravo, rotate your entire squad to Point Alpha or Charlie to force them to split their forces.
3.  **Extraction Baiting:** In the Dark Zone, call for an extraction at one zone but move to another. This lures Rogue Agents away from your actual exit point.

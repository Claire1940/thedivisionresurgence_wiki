## Understanding Gear Level and Power Score

One of the biggest departures from previous entries in the franchise is how gear progression is handled in 2026. In *The Division Resurgence*, gear has an independent level that is separate from your character level. This means you can keep a "God Roll" piece of gear from level 10 and upgrade it all the way to level 40 using resources.

Your total combat effectiveness is represented by your **Power Score**. This number is an aggregate of your weapon levels, gear stats, equipped mods, and even account-wide achievements.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Dismantling gear only returns 90% of the invested materials. Before you dump resources into a new piece of gear, ensure it truly fits your Season 2 build.

### Power Score Benchmarks
To progress through the main campaign and unlock endgame activities, you must meet specific Power Score requirements. If a mission recommends 12.6K Power Score and you enter at 11K, you will face a significant damage penalty.

## Seasonal Activity Priority List

Efficiency is the name of the game in Season 2. The game implements a daily XP boost system (indicated by an orange arrow near your level). Once this boost is exhausted, the arrow turns gray, and your XP gains are significantly reduced. To maximize your time, follow this priority list:

| Activity Type | Reward Value | Daily Limit / Cycle |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Daily Quests** | Very High XP | Fixed Daily Set |
| **Main Campaign** | Extreme XP | Once per mission |
| **Open World Activities** | Moderate Loot | 10 Rewarded Completions |
| **Lone Wolf Challenge** | Seasonal Materials | 21-Day Cycle |
| **Dark Zone Bosses** | High-End Gear | Unlimited |

### The Lone Wolf Challenge
This mode functions similarly to "The Summit" from *The Division 2*. Agents must clear stages within a time limit to earn maximum stars. Every 21 days, the challenge resets with a new "Specialization Focus," providing buffs for using specific classes (e.g., Demolitionist). Completing these stages is one of the best ways to earn high-end OS Protocols.

## Dark Zone Survival Strategies

The Dark Zone remains the most rewarding—and dangerous—area in the game. In Season 2, the Dark Zone features a 20-minute session timer, acting as a mini-extraction shooter within the larger RPG framework.

1.  **Targeted Loot**: At level 20, you can select "Targeted Loot" for your Dark Zone session. This increases the drop rate for specific gear sets or weapon types.
2.  **Extraction Etiquette**: Never call for an extraction alone if you can avoid it. The flare gun alerts every player in the vicinity. Use the "Solo Queue" option if you want to avoid pre-made squads.
3.  **Contaminated Gear**: Remember that any loot found in the Dark Zone must be extracted via helicopter. If you die before the chopper leaves, you lose everything you found in that session.

> **💡 Tip:** Check the damage types of your weapons. In Season 2, enemies often have shields that correspond to specific types: **Blast (Red)**, **Shredding (Light Blue)**, and **Piercing (Dark Blue)**. Matching these is vital for high-level content.

## Crafting and Recalibration in 2026

Crafting in *The Division Resurgence* is a long-term investment. Unlike previous games, crafting items takes real-world time (often 24 hours). However, as you increase your **Crafting Expertise**, your chances of crafting High-End (Gold) items increase significantly.

**Recalibration** unlocks at level 20 and is only available for High-End and Exotic gear. You can reroll one attribute or talent per piece. To get a "God Roll," you should farm for a piece that already has three out of four desired stats, then recalibrate the final slot.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I unlock Season 2 content in The Division Resurgence?**

A: Most seasonal content, including the Lone Wolf Challenge and advanced Dark Zone tiers, unlocks naturally as you progress through the main story. Reaching level 20 is the primary "gate" that opens up recalibration and targeted loot.

**Q: Can I play with friends on different servers?**

A: No. While the game features cross-play between iOS and Android, servers (North America, Europe, Asia) are isolated. Ensure your entire clan starts on the same server to play together, as characters cannot be transferred.

**Q: What is the best way to increase Power Score quickly?**

A: Focus on upgrading your existing gear rather than waiting for new drops. Use your materials to level up your primary weapons and OS Protocols. Additionally, completing SHD Commendations provides account-wide stat buffs that contribute to your overall Power Score.

**Q: Does this the division resurgence season 2 guide apply to solo players?**

A: Yes! While the Dark Zone and Heroic Nests are easier with a group, the Demolitionist specialization and the "Solo Queue" Dark Zone option make it entirely possible to reach the endgame as a solo agent in 2026.

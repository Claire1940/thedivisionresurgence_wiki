## Optimization Tips for Older Devices

If your current smartphone doesn't quite hit the **ディビジョン リサージェンス 推奨スペック**, you can still enjoy the game by making specific adjustments in the settings menu.

*   **Frame Rate Over Graphics:** Always prioritize "Frame Rate" (set to 60 or Max) over "Graphics Quality." A smooth game is more playable than a pretty one.
*   **Shadow Quality:** Turn shadows to "Low." Shadows are one of the most resource-intensive features in the Snowdrop Engine.
*   **Object Detail:** Reduce the draw distance. This prevents the phone from rendering buildings and debris that are too far away to matter.
*   **Post-Processing:** Disable Motion Blur and Depth of Field. These effects add cinematic flair but can obscure vision in competitive play and strain the GPU.

> **💡 Tip:** Use a mobile cooling fan attachment if you plan on playing for more than an hour. Thermal throttling is the primary cause of performance degradation in Division Resurgence.

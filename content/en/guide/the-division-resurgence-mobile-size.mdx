## Navigating the Open World: Factions and Combat

As you explore the virus-stricken streets of New York, you will encounter various factions. Each group has its own AI behavior, models, and weapon sets, further contributing to the overall **the division resurgence mobile size**.

*   **Raiders:** Desperate civilians turned to crime. They are low-threat but often use Molotov cocktails to flush you out of cover.
*   **Rikers:** Escaped convicts seeking revenge against authority. They are aggressive and moderately dangerous.
*   **Cleaners:** Former sanitation workers who believe fire is the only cure for the virus. They deal high burn damage.

To survive these encounters, you must utilize the environment. Shooting red barrels can trigger massive explosions, clearing groups of enemies much faster than traditional headshots. Additionally, do not ignore side missions; while main missions provide the most XP, side quests are faster and essential for leveling up your agent to match the increasing power of your enemies.

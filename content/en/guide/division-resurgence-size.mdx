## How to Manage Storage and Performance

If you find that the **division resurgence size** is pushing your device to its limit, there are several ways to optimize your storage without sacrificing too much of the experience.

*   **Clear Cache Regularly:** Over time, the game accumulates temporary files from the Dark Zone and PvP matches. Clearing the cache via your device settings can often reclaim 500MB to 1GB.
*   **Selective Language Downloads:** If the game offers optional voice packs, only download the language you intend to use. This can save several hundred megabytes.
*   **Offload Unused Apps:** Before a major seasonal update, offload photos to the cloud or delete unused apps to ensure the update process doesn't stall.

> **💡 Tip:** Always perform the initial "In-Game Data Download" while connected to a stable Wi-Fi network. Using mobile data can lead to corrupted packets, which might force the game to re-download files, effectively doubling the data used.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I move The Division Resurgence to an SD card on Android?**

A: While you can move the initial APK to some SD cards, it is highly recommended to keep the main data on your device's internal storage. SD cards often have slower read/write speeds, which can cause significant texture pop-in and long loading times in the open world.

**Q: Why does the division resurgence size increase after every update?**

A: Each update typically adds new story chapters, side quests, seasonal gear, and bug fixes. While developers try to optimize old files, the addition of new high-resolution assets naturally increases the total footprint over time.

**Q: Is there a "Lite" version of the game for older phones?**

A: Currently, there is no official "Lite" version. However, you can lower the graphical settings in the options menu to "Low" or "Performance" mode, which reduces the strain on your hardware, even if the storage size remains the same.

**Q: Does the Dark Zone require a separate download?**

A: No, the Dark Zone is integrated into the main game map. However, the assets for the Dark Zone are part of the large post-install download that occurs when you first launch the app.

## How to Obtain Exotic Items in 2026

Acquiring items from **the division resurgence exotic items list** is a marathon, not a sprint. Unlike standard loot, Exotics rarely drop from common street thugs. You must engage with the game's most challenging content to earn these rewards.

### 1. High-Level Bounties and Side Missions
Bounties are one of the most reliable ways to earn high-end currency and gear. As you progress, heroic-level bounties will offer a small percentage chance to drop Exotic components or fully formed items. Always check your map for daily and weekly bounty resets.

### 2. The Dark Zone (DZ) Extraction
The Dark Zone remains the most dangerous but rewarding area in Manhattan. Exotic items found here are "contaminated" and must be extracted via helicopter. A major update in 2026 allows players to leave the area via the same extraction point, simplifying the exit strategy, but you must still defend your loot from rogue agents until the rope clears.

### 3. Crafting and Expertise
One of the most overlooked aspects of gear progression is the Expertise system. Before you attempt to craft any high-level weapons, ensure you have upgraded your **Weapon and Armor Expertise** for that specific category. Upgrading your expertise at least once significantly increases the chances of crafting a higher-quality version of the item.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Selling lower-rarity items is essential for gold, but never dismantle a potential Exotic component. You will need a massive amount of gold for late-game upgrades, so start saving your currency early.

## Optimizing Your Settings for High-Tier Combat

To effectively farm for **the division resurgence exotic items list**, your technical setup needs to be as sharp as your aim. Mobile gaming presents unique challenges, such as input lag and screen clutter, which can be mitigated with the right configuration.

### Recommended Quality of Life Settings
*   **Gyroscope:** Enable "Scope Only" or "Always On" to give yourself an edge in precision aiming.
*   **ADS Perspective:** Switch this to **First Person**. This provides a cleaner view of the battlefield and makes hitting enemy weak points (like gas canisters) much easier.
*   **Reload Interruption:** Switch this **ON**. This allows you to cancel a reload and start shooting immediately if an enemy surprises you.
*   **Immediate Fire during ADS:** Enable this to remove the delay between pulling your gun up and the first bullet leaving the chamber.
*   **Auto Loot:** Enable the bottom row for auto-loot settings to ensure you never accidentally leave an Epic or Exotic item on the ground.

### Controller vs. Touchscreen
While the touchscreen controls are robust, many top-tier players prefer using a Bluetooth controller, such as a PlayStation 5 or Xbox controller. Using a controller reduces the physical obstruction on your screen, allowing you to see incoming threats more clearly. If you are playing on a monitor via a USB-C to HDMI adapter, a controller is almost mandatory for a console-like experience with zero latency.

## Advanced Combat Tactics for Exotic Hunting

Having the items from **the division resurgence exotic items list** is only half the battle; you must also know how to use them. Enemy AI in Resurgence is tactical, often using cover and flanking maneuvers to pin you down.

### Target Weak Points
Headshots are excellent for damage, but targeting weak points is often more effective for crowd control. Most heavy enemies have gas canisters or ammo belts on their backs. Destroying these can cause massive explosions that damage surrounding enemies and stun the target.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I trade items from the division resurgence exotic items list with my friends?**

A: Currently, items can only be shared with squad members who were present when the item dropped. This sharing window usually lasts for two hours. Permanent trading between players is not a feature in the 2026 build to prevent "black market" item sales.

**Q: What is the fastest way to get Exotic components?**

A: The fastest way is to dismantle unwanted Exotic items or to complete weekly legendary projects. These projects often require you to donate a certain amount of resources or complete specific missions in exchange for an Exotic Cache.

**Q: Does the "Immediate Fire" setting affect my accuracy?**

A: It does not directly lower your accuracy stats, but it can make recoil management slightly harder if you aren't prepared for the gun to fire the moment you aim. It is highly recommended for close-to-mid-range builds.

**Q: Are there any time-limited Exotics?**

A: Some items on **the division resurgence exotic items list** are tied to seasonal Battle Passes or limited-time events. While these items usually enter the general loot pool after the season ends, the easiest way to get them is during their featured event.

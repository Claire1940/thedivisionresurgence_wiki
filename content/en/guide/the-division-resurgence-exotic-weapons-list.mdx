### Lady Death (SMG)
A fan favorite from *The Division 2*, the Lady Death makes a triumphant return in the mobile version. Its talent, **Breath Freely**, rewards players who stay in constant motion. You gain stacks while moving and even more while sprinting. Each shot consumes these stacks to provide a massive 15% boost to weapon damage. Additionally, kills grant a movement speed buff, making it the premier choice for aggressive "strafing" builds.

### Warlord (Assault Rifle)
The Warlord is an "OG" weapon that traces its roots back to the original game. Its talent, **Brutal Treatment**, is designed for "tanky" players who aren't afraid to stand their ground. While firing, you take 25% less damage. However, 60% of that reduced damage is stored and applied to you once you stop firing. In the 2026 version of Resurgence, this talent also provides a skill damage bonus after you stop shooting, allowing for a hybrid playstyle between gunplay and gadget usage.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Be careful when using the Warlord in high-intensity firefights. If you store too much damage, the "delayed" hit you take after you stop firing can down you instantly if your health is low.

### Scorpio (Shotgun)
The Scorpio remains one of the best utility weapons in the game. By hitting targets, you apply debuffs that stack up to seven times. These range from Bleed and Confusion to the highly coveted Shock effect at six stacks. At the maximum of seven stacks, the target takes 10% additional damage from all sources. This makes the Scorpio a must-have for team-based legendary missions.

## Beyond Exotics: The Standard Arsenal

While **the division resurgence exotic weapons list** is what most players chase, the backbone of your loadout will often consist of high-end rifles, shotguns, and SMGs. The game features over 90 distinct weapon models, many of which will be familiar to veterans of the franchise.

### Assault Rifles and Rifles
Assault Rifles (ARs) are the most versatile class in the game, offering a balance of range, fire rate, and damage. Rifles, on the other hand, are semi-automatic and reward precision.

*   **AR Highlights:** Military 416, AK-47, G36C, ACR, and the classic Police M4.
*   **Rifle Highlights:** M1A, Urban MDR, M1 Grand (with a bayonet), and the MK17.

For those who prefer to stay back, the Marksman Rifle category offers massive burst damage. The **M700 Carbon** and the **SVD** remain staples for high-damage sniper builds. New additions and variations like the **DSR-1** and the **L15 A3** provide a variety of bolt-action and semi-auto options for different engagement distances.

## Customizing Your Loadout

Finding a weapon from **the division resurgence exotic weapons list** is only the first step. To truly excel, you must pair these weapons with the right gear sets and skills. For example, the **Diamondback** (referred to as the "Down Back" in some early beta translations) marks a random enemy. Hitting that enemy guarantees a critical hit. To maximize this, players should look for gear that increases Critical Hit Damage, as the chance is already guaranteed by the weapon's talent.

### Weapon Talents and Synergy
In Resurgence, weapon talents often interact with your skills. The Lady Death, for instance, consumes stacks to increase skill damage by 15%. This creates a powerful loop where you move to build stacks, shoot to soften targets, and then deploy a high-damage Seeker Mine or Turret to finish the job.

> **💡 Tip:** Always check the "Talent" section of your weapon. Even non-exotic weapons have randomized talents that can significantly boost your DPS if they align with your character's specialization.

## How to Obtain Exotic Weapons

As of 2026, obtaining items from **the division resurgence exotic weapons list** involves several high-level activities. While some are quest-related, most require consistent participation in the game's toughest content.

1.  **Dark Zone Extraction:** The Dark Zone remains the most lucrative source for high-tier loot, including Exotics. However, you must successfully extract the item to keep it.
2.  **Weekly Projects:** Completing a series of difficult tasks often rewards an Exotic Cache.
3.  **Legendary Missions:** These are the ultimate test of skill, offering a small percentage chance for an Exotic drop upon completion.
4.  **Special Events:** Ubisoft frequently runs limited-time events where specific Exotics have a higher drop rate.

For more information on official drop rates and upcoming events, visit the [official Ubisoft Resurgence site](https://www.ubisoft.com/en-us/game/the-division/resurgence).

## FAQ

**Q: Can I equip more than one exotic weapon at a time?**

A: No, standard Division rules apply in Resurgence. You can generally only equip one Exotic weapon and one Exotic piece of armor at any given time to prevent power creep.

**Q: Is the Diamondback good for solo players?**

A: Yes, the Diamondback is excellent for solo play because it guarantees critical hits, allowing you to spec your gear into pure damage or survivability without worrying about critical hit chance.

**Q: Where can I find the most updated the division resurgence exotic weapons list?**

A: While this guide covers the core Exotics available in 2026, the game is frequently updated. Always check your in-game "Collections" tab to see if new orange-tier items have been added to the loot pool.

**Q: Does the Tommy Gun require a specific build?**

A: The Tommy Gun's "Carefree" talent focuses entirely on hip-fire. To make this weapon viable, you should use gear that increases your "Accuracy while moving" and "Hip-fire stability" to ensure your shots actually land.

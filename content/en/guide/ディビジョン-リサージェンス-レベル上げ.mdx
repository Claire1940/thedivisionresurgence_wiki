### Main Mission Efficiency
The cornerstone of **ディビジョン リサージェンス レベル上げ** is the main campaign. These missions are designed to provide enough experience to keep you moving through the map's sectors. If you find yourself struggling with a mission's difficulty, it is usually better to run it on "Normal" difficulty rather than "Hard" for your first playthrough. While Hard mode offers slightly more XP per kill, the increased time spent in combat often results in a lower overall XP-per-hour rate.

> **💡 Tip:** Always check the recommended level for a mission. You can usually start a mission if you are within 2 levels of the requirement, but the difficulty spike may slow down your clearing speed significantly.

## Open World Progression and Side Missions

Once you unlock the Base of Operations, the map will begin to populate with various icons. To maintain a steady **ディビジョン リサージェンス レベル上げ** pace, you should focus on clearing side missions within your current level bracket before moving to a new district.

1.  **Unlock Safe Houses**: Every new district has a Safe House. Entering these and checking the Situation Board reveals all local side missions and encounters.
2.  **Strategic Clearances**: Side missions in Resurgence often reward a percentage of the XP required for your next level, meaning they remain relevant even if you tackle them later.
3.  **Encounters**: These are short, objective-based tasks that provide resources for your Base of Operations wings (Medical, Tech, and Security). Upgrading these wings is essential for unlocking the skills that make leveling easier.

### Recommended Stat Distribution
For a smooth leveling experience, don't focus solely on DPS (Damage Per Second). A "glass cannon" build often leads to frequent deaths, which resets mission progress and wastes time. Instead, aim for a balanced distribution that favors survivability during the **ディビジョン リサージェンス レベル上げ** phase.

*   **Toughness (Stamina)**: Your primary stat. High HP allows you to stay out of cover longer to finish off enemies.
*   **Skill Power (Electronics)**: Important for the potency of your healing skills (like First Aid) and seeker mines.
*   **Firearms (DPS)**: Secondary to survivability until you reach the endgame where specialized builds become viable.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not spend your credits on low-level gear at vendors. You will outgrow equipment every 2–3 levels. Save your currency for recalibration and high-end blueprints at level 30.

## Dark Zone Leveling vs. Light Zone Leveling

The Dark Zone (DZ) has its own separate leveling system. While it doesn't directly increase your main level, the gear you find there will make the **ディビジョン リサージェンス レベル上げ** in the "Light Zone" (the standard map) much faster. 

| Feature | Light Zone (Story) | Dark Zone (PvPvE) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Risk Factor** | Low | High (Player Killers) |
| **XP Type** | Main Level | DZ Rank |
| **Loot Quality** | Standard | High/Contaminated |
| **Best Strategy** | Missions & Projects | Landmark Clearing |

The most efficient way to handle the DZ is to enter with a full squad. Solo players are often targeted by "Rogue" agents, which can lead to losing your contaminated loot and slowing down your progression.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way for ディビジョン リサージェンス レベル上げ in solo play?**

A: The fastest solo method is to prioritize Main Missions until you hit a level gap, then clear all "Side Missions" in the highest-level district you can safely survive. Avoid "Encounters" unless you need specific wing resources, as their XP yield is lower.

**Q: Should I dismantle or sell my old gear?**

A: During the early levels (1–20), it is generally better to sell gear to accumulate credits. From level 20–30, start dismantling gear to gather crafting materials, as you will need these to craft high-end mods and weapons once you reach the level cap.

**Q: Does playing in a group increase the XP I earn?**

A: While the base XP for mission completion remains the same, playing in a group allows you to clear missions much faster and handle higher difficulties. This increases your "XP per hour," making group play the superior choice for rapid leveling.

**Q: How do I unlock Specializations?**

A: Specializations typically unlock after reaching level 30 and completing the main story campaign. Once unlocked, you can earn specialized XP to upgrade their unique talent trees.

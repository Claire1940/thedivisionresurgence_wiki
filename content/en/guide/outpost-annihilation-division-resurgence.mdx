Outpost Annihilation is a multi-stage world event that triggers in various districts of Manhattan, including iconic locations like Times Square and Liberty Island. ## Facing the Freeman Faction

The Freemen are distinct from the Rikers or Cleaners seen in previous entries. They are highly organized survivors who use scavenged industrial tools—like nail guns, circular saws, and modified drones—to defend their territory. In **outpost annihilation division resurgence** missions, you will encounter specific unit types that require unique tactics.

*   **The Welder:** A heavy-armored unit that uses a flamethrower-like torch. Aim for the fuel tanks on their back to cause an explosion.
*   **The Drone Operator:** Stays in the backline and deploys "Scrap Drones" that seek out Agents behind cover. These must be prioritized by your team's sharpshooters.
*   **The Foreman:** The final boss of an outpost. They usually possess unique mechanics, such as deploying localized EMP fields or calling in reinforcements from nearby buildings.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Freeman "Rushers" carry improvised explosives. If they get within five meters of your position, they will detonate, causing massive bleed damage and knocking you out of cover.

## Tactical Strategies for Success

To consistently clear outposts on Heroic difficulty, follow these steps:

1.  **Scout the Perimeter:** Use your pulse skill to identify the location of the Foreman and any hidden turrets before the first shot is fired.
2.  **Focus Fire:** Coordinate with your team to focus fire on one elite unit at a time. A common mistake is splitting damage across multiple targets, allowing them to retreat and heal.
3.  **Manage the Flanks:** In **outpost annihilation division resurgence**, the AI is aggressive. If you stay in one cover for too long, the Freemen will send Rushers to your left and right. Rotate your position every 60 seconds.
4.  **Use the Environment:** Many outposts contain explosive barrels or hanging crates. Shooting these at the right moment can wipe out an entire wave of reinforcements instantly.

> **💡 Tip:** If you are playing on a mobile device without a controller, enable the "Tap-to-Cover" option in the settings to improve your mobility during intense firefights.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I solo the Outpost Annihilation missions?**

A: While Normal and Hard difficulties can be completed solo with a high enough gear score, Heroic and Legendary difficulties are designed for a full squad of four Agents. The enemy scaling makes it very difficult to manage aggro alone.

**Q: Do Outpost Annihilation rewards contribute to my Division 2 progress?**

A: Yes! Through the Ubisoft Connect integration, completing milestones in **outpost annihilation division resurgence** can unlock the "Resurgence Agent Pack" and other cosmetics in The Division 2. Reaching Level 10 in Resurgence also grants a Level 40 Character Boost for the PC/console game.

**Q: What is the best weapon type for fighting the Freemen?**

A: Assault Rifles and LMGs are generally the most effective. Assault Rifles provide the balance needed for mid-range cover combat, while LMGs are excellent for "suppressing" Freeman units, preventing them from moving while your teammates flank.

**Q: How often do the outposts reset?**

A: Outposts in the open world typically reset every 24 hours. However, during special "Global Events," they may reset more frequently or offer double rewards for a limited time. Always check your map for the "Annihilation" icon to see which zones are currently active.

## Understanding the Specialization System

In Division Resurgence, "Specializations" (専門職) act as the game's class system. Each role comes with a fixed Signature Ability (Ultimate), a signature weapon, and two specific combat skills. Unlike previous titles where skills were highly flexible, Resurgence ties specific gadgets to specific roles to encourage team synergy.

> **⚠️ Warning:** You choose your first specialization during the tutorial. You cannot change your role until you reach **Level 12**. Choose wisely to avoid a difficult early-game grind.

## Tactical Advice for Solo vs. Group Play

When playing alone, the **ディビジョン リサージェンス 専門職 おすすめ** is typically the **Demolitionist**. The Seeker Mine acts as a secondary "player," hunting down enemies while you focus on high-priority targets. The ability to destroy cover with the Grenade Launcher is invaluable when you don't have teammates to flank the enemy.

However, in a four-man squad, the **Vanguard** becomes incredibly powerful. The Vanguard’s "Scan Pulse" highlights every enemy on the map for your teammates, providing a massive tactical advantage in the dark corridors of the New York subway systems. Furthermore, their "Tactical Link" provides a damage buff to everyone nearby, which is essential for melting boss health bars in 2026's endgame content.

> **💡 Tip:** If you find yourself dying too often in solo play, try switching to the **Bulwark**. The Phalanx Shield provides a massive safety net, allowing you to retreat while still facing the enemy.

## How to Change and Unlock New Specializations

As mentioned earlier, you are locked into your first choice until Level 12. Once you reach this milestone, you can visit the SHD Specialist NPC in the main Hub (The Base of Operations) to unlock and switch between roles.

1.  Reach **Level 12** by completing main story missions.
2.  Complete the "Specialization Introduction" side quest.
3.  Open the Character Menu and navigate to the "Specialization" tab.
4.  Spend Specialization Points (earned through leveling) to upgrade your skills.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the best ディビジョン リサージェンス 専門職 おすすめ for a total beginner?**

A: The **Demolitionist** is widely considered the best for beginners. Its skills are "set and forget," and the Grenade Launcher provides an easy way to deal with difficult bosses without needing perfect aim.

**Q: Can I master all specializations on one character?**

A: Yes. While you can only have one active at a time, you can eventually unlock and level up every specialization on a single character. This allows you to fill different roles depending on what your team needs.

**Q: Is the Technical Operator better than the Demolitionist for DPS?**

A: The Technical Operator focuses more on sustained damage through drones and turrets, whereas the Demolitionist focuses on burst damage. For most players, the Demolitionist feels more powerful because of its immediate impact on the battlefield.

**Q: Does my choice of specialization affect which weapons I can use?**

A: You can use any standard weapon (Assault Rifles, SMGs, etc.) regardless of your specialization. However, each role has passive bonuses for specific weapon types. For example, the Bulwark often receives bonuses for Shotguns and SMGs, while the Vanguard might favor Marksman Rifles.

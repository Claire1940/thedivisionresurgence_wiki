## Troubleshooting Post-Maintenance Issues

Occasionally, following a major update in **the division 2 server maintenance schedule april 2026**, players may encounter specific error codes. The most common are Delta-03 and Mike-01.

1.  **Delta-03:** This indicates a loss of connection to the server. If this happens immediately after maintenance, the servers may still be in "limited access" mode. Wait 10 minutes and try again.
2.  **Mike-01:** This error means the game cannot reach the profile service. This is common if the maintenance has been extended or if your local network is blocking the new update's handshake.
3.  **Echo-01:** Usually related to server-side congestion. If thousands of players try to log in the second the servers go live, the Echo error will trigger as a "queue" mechanism.

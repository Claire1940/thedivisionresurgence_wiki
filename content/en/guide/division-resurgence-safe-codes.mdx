## Detailed Walkthrough: The Serotentia Birthday Gift Safe

The safe located near the Madison Field Hospital in the Serotentia area is unique because it requires a multi-step process. Unlike other safes where the code is written on a wall, this one involves a collectible item.

1.  **Locate the Collectible:** Before entering the building, look for a glowing electronic device on the ground. Picking this up adds a "Code Clue" to your inventory.
2.  **Read the Clue:** The clue mentions a "Birthday Gift" and lists two dates: Tuesday, 11th of May, or 5th of November.
3.  **Input the Code:** For the US-style date format used in the game, the correct combination is **1105**.
4.  **Claim the Reward:** This safe typically offers a higher tier of rewards compared to the basic shop safes found earlier in the game.

## How to Decipher Cryptic Clues

There are currently 11 "Cryptic Clues" listed in the collectibles menu of *The Division Resurgence*. While we have covered the most common **division resurgence safe codes** above, new ones are discovered as players push into higher-level Dark Zones and Conflict areas.

When you encounter a new safe, follow this checklist:
1.  **Check the Walls:** Look for chalkboards or posters with four-digit numbers.
2.  **Look at Dates:** If the safe is in a business, look for "Since [Year]" or "Established [Year]" signs.
3.  **Check the Floor:** Often, a "Portable Electronic Device" (PED) collectible will be lying nearby with a text log containing the hint.
4.  **Check the Map:** Sometimes the safe code is part of the street address or a room number indicated on local maps.

## FAQ

**Q: Are division resurgence safe codes the same for every player?**

A: Yes, these codes are static and do not change between accounts or sessions. Once a code is discovered, it can be used by any player at that specific location.

**Q: Can I open a safe more than once?**

A: No, safes are generally one-time rewards per character. Once you have successfully entered the code and looted the contents, the safe will remain open and cannot be reset.

**Q: What should I do if the code isn't working?**

A: Double-check that you are at the correct location. Some safes look identical but are tied to different areas. Also, ensure you are inputting the numbers exactly as shown, as some interaction menus can be sensitive to rapid tapping.

**Q: Do I need to find the clue before I can enter the code?**

A: In most cases, you can enter the code even if you haven't technically "found" the clue in-game. However, for safes tied to specific collectibles (like the Serotentia safe), you may need to pick up the item first to trigger the interaction.

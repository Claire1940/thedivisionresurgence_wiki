## A-Tier: Reliable Workhorse Weapons

A-Tier weapons are fantastic alternatives if you haven't yet secured an S-Tier "God Roll." These firearms are highly effective for World Tier 5 content and can comfortably carry a team through most Incursions.

*   **ACR (Assault Rifle):** While it has a slightly lower fire rate than the M4, its exceptional handling makes it a favorite for players who struggle with recoil management.
*   **Vector 45 ACP (SMG):** Known for its blistering rate of fire, it can "melt" enemies at point-blank range instantly. However, be prepared for frequent reloads due to its small magazine capacity.
*   **Custom M44 (Sniper):** Preferred by Dark Zone players seeking maximum "one-tap" potential. It scales incredibly well with high Firearms stats.
*   **Big Alejandro (LMG):** An exotic LMG that rewards defensive play. Its fire rate and damage increase while you are in cover, making it the king of stationary suppression.

## Essential Weapon Talents for PvE and PvP

In the world of **ディビジョン リサージェンス おすすめ武器**, the raw damage numbers on your screen are only half the story. To truly optimize your build, your weapon talents must align with your core stats: Firearms, Toughness, and Electronics.

### Top PvE Talents for High-Level Content
For those focusing on Incursions like "Dragon's Nest" or "Falcon Lost," efficiency against armored targets is mandatory.

1.  **Destructive:** Increases enemy armor damage. Since almost all endgame enemies are heavily armored, this is a non-negotiable talent.
2.  **Ferocious:** Increases damage against Elite and Named enemies. This is essential for boss encounters.
3.  **Predatory:** Killing a target regenerates health over time. This is a vital survival tool for solo players and front-line rushers.

### Top PvP Talents for the Dark Zone
When entering the Dark Zone, the priority shifts toward burst damage and utility to win quick 1v1 engagements.

| Talent | Effect | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Responsive** | Damage increases when close to the target | Essential for SMG rushers and shotgun builds. |
| **Unforgiving** | Damage increases as your health drops | A high-risk, high-reward talent that wins duels. |
| **Competent** | Weapon damage increases after using a skill | Pairs perfectly with Pulse or First Aid for a burst window. |

## Weapon Class Rankings and Synergy

The choice of **ディビジョン リサージェンス おすすめ武器** should always be influenced by your chosen Gear Set. A top-tier weapon used with an incompatible set will result in mediocre performance.

### Assault Rifles (AR)
Assault Rifles remain the most popular class in 2026 due to their innate "Enemy Armor Damage" bonus. The **Lightweight M4** remains the king of the **ディビジョン リサージェンス おすすめ武器** AR rankings, but the exotic **Bullfrog** is a close second for players who can master its aggressive horizontal kick.

### Submachine Guns (SMG)
SMGs come with an innate Critical Hit Chance bonus, making them the primary choice for "Striker" or "D3-FNC" builds. While **The House** is the undisputed champion, the **Aug A3** offers a more stable alternative for those who find the Vector's recoil too erratic for mid-range skirmishes.

### Marksman Rifles (Snipers)
If you prefer the "Sentry's Call" or "DeadEYE" playstyles, the **M700 Carbon** and **Custom M44** are the only two snipers worth considering for serious endgame play. They offer the best scaling with Firearms stats.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid using high-capacity "spam" snipers like the SCAR-H in PvP. They lack the burst damage required to down an enemy before they can use a medkit.

## How to Farm Top-Tier Weapons in 2026

Acquiring the best **ディビジョン リサージェンス おすすめ武器** requires a strategic approach to farming. In 2026, the most efficient methods involve high-difficulty activities and time-limited events.

*   **Resistance Mode:** This horde-based mode is widely considered the best way to farm "The House" and other top-tier exotics by opening the reward crates after Wave 10.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the single best weapon for a beginner in The Division Resurgence?**

A: The **Lightweight M4** is widely considered the best starting point. Its predictable recoil and high fire rate make it forgiving for new players while remaining powerful enough for the endgame.

**Q: How do I reroll bad talents on my weapons?**

A: Visit the "Recalibration Station" in the Base of Operations. You can spend Weapon Parts and Phoenix Credits to change one talent on a High-End weapon. Note that once you change one, the other two become locked.

**Q: Is Gear Score more important than weapon talents?**

A: No. A lower Gear Score weapon with perfect talents (like Destructive and Ferocious) will almost always outperform a max Gear Score weapon with useless talents. You can use the "Optimization Station" later to bring your "God Roll" weapon up to max level.

**Q: Can I find Exotics outside of the Dark Zone?**

A: Yes. In the 2026 meta, Exotics drop from Weekly Caches, Legendary Mission rewards, Resistance crates, and even Open World bosses. The Dark Zone is simply one of the fastest—and most dangerous—ways to acquire them.

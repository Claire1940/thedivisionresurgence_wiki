### 1. Spring Blossom Settlement Safe
The Spring Blossom Settlement is one of the first major hubs you will encounter. To find this safe, fast travel to the area and head toward the upper levels of the main building. Follow the path to the top floor until you find a small office room.

*   **Location:** Upper room, Spring Blossom Settlement.

*   **Location:** Inside the BoO Dormitories.

*   **Code:** **1350**

### 3. Oak Lock Store (Near BoO)
Just outside the main entrance of the Base of Operations, take a right and run down the street. You will see a storefront labeled "Oak Lock." Inside, a small safe sits on the floor behind the counter.

*   **Location:** Oak Lock retail store, street level.
*   **Clue:** Look at the promotional sign near the entrance.
*   **Code:** **122**

### 4. The Supermarket Safe
Directly across the street from the Oak Lock store is a large supermarket. This area is often filled with low-level NPCs, so clear them out before attempting to crack the safe located along the back wall.

*   **Location:** Back wall of the Supermarket.
*   **Clue:** A large sign hanging above the safe area.

This is a "Large Safe" and requires a multi-step process. Located near the Madison Field Hospital (a callback to the original Division), you must first find a collectible on the ground outside the Serotentia building.

*   **Location:** Inside the Serotentia building near Madison Field Hospital.
*   **Clue:** Collectible titled "Birthday Gift." The clue mentions "11th of May" or "5th of November."
*   **Code:** **1105**

### 6. Autumn's Hope Safe House
The Autumn's Hope safe house contains a safe right next to the reception desk. This one is notoriously difficult to see because the clue is integrated into the "Established" date on the wall signage behind the NPC Mullins.

*   **Location:** Reception area, Autumn's Hope.
*   **Clue:** The "Established" year on the wall sign.
*   **Code:** **1883**

### 7. Dante's Run Safe House
At the Dante's Run safe house, exit the main spawn room and head toward the clothing store area (look for the mannequins). The safe is tucked behind the main checkout desk.

*   **Location:** Clothing store section of Dante's Run.
*   **Clue:** A poster on the front of the desk.
*   **Code:** **7716**

### 8. Kerman Station Safe House
Kerman Station is a vital hub for mid-game progression. To find the safe here, exit the main room and head toward the "Safia Nerdies" lockers. The code is conveniently pinned to the locker door.

*   **Location:** Locker area, Kerman Station.
*   **Clue:** Note pinned next to the lockers.
*   **Code:** **7069**

## FAQ

A: Environmental safe codes can be used by every player, but each character can only loot a specific safe once. Promotional redeem codes are typically one-time use per account.

**Q: Why isn't the safe code working for me?**

A: Ensure you are at the correct safe. Some safes look identical but belong to different districts. Also, double-check that you haven't already looted the safe, as the prompt may still appear even if the rewards have been claimed.

**Q: Where can I find more codes for 2026?**

A: New codes are frequently discovered by the community on Discord and Reddit. Additionally, Ubisoft often hides codes in their "Intelligence Annex" blog posts on the official game site.

**Q: Do I need to find the clue before I can enter the code?**

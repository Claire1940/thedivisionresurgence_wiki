## Storage and Installation Details

One of the most overlooked aspects of the **ディビジョン リサージェンス スペック** is the storage footprint. Based on data from the final beta phases leading into 2026, the initial download is only the beginning. Once the game is installed, it fetches additional high-resolution textures and map data.

*   **Initial Download:** Approximately 2-4 GB.
*   **Full Installation:** Between 10 GB and 15 GB depending on the device's texture pack requirements.
*   **Future Updates:** We recommend keeping at least 20 GB of free space to accommodate seasonal content patches and map expansions.

Because the game utilizes Unreal Engine 4 to render a seamless New York City, the speed of your storage also matters. Devices with UFS 3.1 or NVMe storage will experience significantly faster loading times when fast-traveling between safe houses.

## Optimizing for 60 FPS Performance

Achieving a smooth 60 FPS is vital for a shooter where every millisecond counts. If your device meets the recommended **ディビジョン リサージェンス スペック** but you still experience stuttering, consider adjusting the following settings in the options menu:

1.  **Shadow Quality:** Lowering this to "Medium" provides the biggest performance boost without sacrificing too much visual clarity.
2.  **Particle Effects:** In heavy combat with multiple explosions, high particle counts can tank the frame rate. Set this to "Balanced."
3.  **Object Detail Distance:** Reducing the draw distance for non-essential objects can help older CPUs maintain stability.
4.  **Anti-Aliasing:** Use "FXAA" or "Low TAA" if you are on a high-resolution tablet screen to save on GPU cycles.

> **Warning:** Running the game at 60 FPS on "Ultra" settings will drain your battery significantly faster and generate more heat. Always monitor your device's temperature to protect your hardware's longevity.

## Network Requirements for Online Play

Since The Division Resurgence is a persistent online world, your hardware specs are only half the battle. To ensure a lag-free experience, your network connection must meet certain criteria.

*   **Minimum Bandwidth:** 5 Mbps download / 1 Mbps upload.
*   **Recommended Bandwidth:** 20+ Mbps for smooth asset streaming.
*   **Ping/Latency:** Under 100ms is ideal for PvE; under 50ms is highly recommended for the Dark Zone and Conflict modes.

Using a 5GHz Wi-Fi connection or a stable 5G cellular signal is preferred over 2.4GHz Wi-Fi, which is prone to interference from other household electronics.

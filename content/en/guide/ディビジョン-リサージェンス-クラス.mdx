## Understanding Specializations in Division Resurgence

In Division Resurgence, "Classes" are referred to as Specializations. Each specialization defines your role within a squad and grants access to a unique Signature Weapon and specific active skills. The game is designed to be fluid; you are not locked into a single path for the entirety of your journey. You can swap your **ディビジョン リサージェンス クラス** at designated hubs, allowing you to experiment with different tactical approaches depending on the mission at hand.

As of April 2026, there are four primary specializations available at launch, each catering to a distinct playstyle:

| Specialization | Primary Role | Signature Weapon | Core Philosophy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Demolitionist** | Area Damage (AOE) | M32A1 Grenade Launcher | Chaos and crowd control through explosives. |
| **Bulwark** | Tanking / Defense | Shield & Specialized Pistol | Leading the charge and absorbing heavy fire. |
| **Field Medic** | Support / Healing | Reinforcer Launcher | Keeping the squad alive in prolonged firefights. |
| **Vanguard** | Recon / Tactical | Custom Tactical Bow | Identifying threats and providing precision fire. |

> **💡 Tip:** While it is tempting to focus solely on damage, having at least one defensive or support class in a co-op team is often the difference between victory and a total wipe.

## Deep Dive into the ディビジョン リサージェンス クラス Options

### The Demolitionist: Master of Explosives
The Demolitionist is the go-to **ディビジョン リサージェンス クラス** for players who enjoy seeing high numbers on their screen. This class excels at flushing enemies out of cover and dealing massive damage to clustered groups. Their Signature Weapon, the M32A1 Grenade Launcher, can clear a room of "Freemen" or other hostile factions in seconds.

*   **Key Skills:** Seekermines and Artillery Turrets.
*   **Best For:** Clearing World Activities and Skirmish PvP modes.
*   **Synergy:** Pairs exceptionally well with a Vanguard who can mark targets for the Demolitionist to incinerate.

### The Bulwark: The Unstoppable Force
If you prefer to be the shield for your team, the Bulwark is your ideal **ディビジョン リサージェンス クラス**. Equipped with a heavy ballistic shield, this class can push into enemy territory, drawing aggro and allowing glass-cannon teammates to fire safely from the rear.

*   **Key Skills:** Deflector Shield and Kinetic Armor.
*   **Best For:** Boss encounters and high-difficulty Dark Zone extractions.
*   **Constraint:** Your mobility is significantly reduced while the shield is active, making positioning vital.

### The Field Medic: The Backbone of the SHD
The 2026 meta has shown that the Field Medic is perhaps the most sought-after **ディビジョン リサージェンス クラス** for endgame content. Their ability to deploy healing mists and revive teammates from a distance ensures that the mission continues even when things go south.

*   **Key Skills:** Healing Hive and Chem Launcher.
*   **Best For:** Raids and 4-player Co-op missions.
*   **Stat Priority:** Focus on "Skill Tier" and "Skill Haste" to keep your healing abilities active as often as possible.

### The Vanguard: Tactical Intelligence
The Vanguard is a specialized **ディビジョン リサージェンス クラス** that focuses on reconnaissance. By using their tactical bow, they can pulse the battlefield, highlighting enemies through walls and providing critical debuffs to high-value targets.

*   **Key Skills:** Pulse Sensor and Recon Drone.
*   **Best For:** PvP (Dominion) and stealth-oriented PvE missions.
*   **Benefit:** Increases the critical hit chance for the entire squad against marked targets.

## How to Unlock and Switch Classes

Progression in Division Resurgence is tied to your SHD Level and story advancement. You will typically unlock your first **ディビジョン リサージェンス クラス** shortly after reaching the main base of operations in Manhattan. 

1.  **Reach Level 10:** Complete the initial prologue and the first few story arcs to unlock the Specialization menu.
2.  **Complete Specialization Tutorials:** Each class has a brief introductory mission that explains its unique mechanics.
3.  **Visit the Quartermaster:** To switch your class, return to the SHD Hub and interact with the Quartermaster NPC.
4.  **Invest Skill Points:** As you level up, you earn points specific to your active specialization. Use these to upgrade your Signature Weapon's damage and cooldowns.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Switching classes does not transfer your skill point investment. You must level up each specialization independently to reach its full potential.

| Class | Solo Play | Team Play | PvP Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Demolitionist** | High | Very High | Medium |
| **Bulwark** | Very High | High | Low |
| **Field Medic** | Medium | Elite | High |

For solo players, the **Bulwark** and **Demolitionist** offer the best balance of survivability and damage. The Bulwark's shield allows you to survive mistakes that would otherwise result in a respawn, while the Demolitionist can end fights before the enemy has a chance to react.

In competitive PvP modes like "Skirmish" or the "Dark Zone," the **Vanguard** is indispensable. Knowledge is power in the Dark Zone, and being able to see an extraction ambush before it happens is the most valuable skill an agent can possess.

## FAQ

**Q: Which ディビジョン リサージェンス クラス is the best for a total beginner?**

A: The **Bulwark** is generally considered the most beginner-friendly class. The added protection of the ballistic shield provides a safety net while you learn the cover-based shooting mechanics and enemy patterns.

**Q: Can I unlock all specializations on a single character?**

A: Yes, you can unlock every **ディビジョン リサージェンス クラス** on one character. You are encouraged to switch between them to suit the needs of your squad or the specific challenges of a mission.

**Q: How do I get more ammo for my Signature Weapon?**

A: Signature ammo drops from powerful enemies (Elites and Named Bosses) or can be replenished at restocks in safe houses. Performing specific actions tied to your class (like getting explosive kills as a Demolitionist) also increases the drop rate of this rare ammo.

**Q: Is there a "Healer" class in Division Resurgence?**

A: Yes, the **Field Medic** functions as the primary healer. It is an essential **ディビジョン リサージェンス クラス** for endgame content, providing both active healing and passive buffs to squad survivability.

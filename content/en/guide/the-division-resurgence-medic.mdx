## The Field Medic Role Overview

The Field Medic is a specialization designed for players who enjoy a hybrid playstyle. Unlike traditional MMO healers, the Medic in Resurgence focuses heavily on "corrosive" damage, which weakens enemy armor and deals damage over time. This makes the role indispensable for boss fights where high-armor targets can otherwise stall a team's progress.

As a veteran of the battlefield, the Field Medic utilizes Hives and Chemical Traps to control the flow of combat. You have the choice to play aggressively by attaching drones to yourself or defensively by tethering your technology to an ally who is pushing the front lines.

## Signature Ability: Oxidizing Swarm

The centerpiece of **the division resurgence medic** toolkit is the Oxidizing Swarm. This is an evolved version of the standard Oxidizing Hive, specifically modified to carry a much higher concentration of corrosive micro-drones. When activated, the Swarm releases a cloud of drones that seek out every enemy within its massive radius.

### How the Oxidizing Swarm Functions
The signature ability does not use standard ammunition; instead, it charges over time as you deal damage to enemies. This encourages the Medic to stay active in the fight rather than hiding.

*   **Deployment:** You can tap the signature button to drop the device at your feet for immediate self-defense, or hold and release to launch it at a distant group of enemies.
*   **Impact:** Upon landing, the micro-drones latch onto targets, applying a potent corrosion effect that eats through health bars and armor segments simultaneously.
*   **Charging:** In the 2026 meta, maximizing your "Skill Haste" through gear attributes is the fastest way to ensure your Swarm is ready for every major encounter.

> **💡 Tip:** Save your Oxidizing Swarm for "Yellow Bar" elite enemies or bosses. The armor-shredding capability is wasted on low-level red-bar enemies that die quickly to standard gunfire.

## Core Specialization Skills

To be an effective **the division resurgence medic**, you must master the two primary skills provided by the specialization tree. These skills offer flexibility depending on whether you are playing solo or in a full four-person squad.

### 1. The Oxidizing Hive
This is your bread-and-butter utility skill. It contains several micro-drones filled with corrosive substances. What makes this version unique in *The Division Resurgence* is its "Follow" mechanic.

| Deployment Mode | How it Works | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Self-Attached** | Tap the skill to keep the Hive on your backpack. | Solo play or when flanking enemies. |
| **Ally-Attached** | Aim and throw the Hive at a teammate. | Supporting a Tank or SMG-rusher. |
| **Trigger** | Drones fly out every time the "host" hits a target. | Focus-firing high-priority targets. |

### 2. The Chemical Trap
The Chemical Trap is a tactical projectile that splits into multiple small containers upon impact. It creates a "Corrosive Zone" on the ground that serves two purposes:
1.  **Enemy Debuff:** Any enemy standing in the zone takes continuous corrosive damage.
2.  **Teammate Buff:** Allies standing within the same zone receive a small, consistent healing effect.

This skill is perfect for "choke points" in missions like the "Water is Life" filtration plant, where enemies often funnel through narrow doorways or hallways.

## Progression and Customization in 2026

As you progress through the game and earn specialization points, you can further customize your **the division resurgence medic** build. While the base kit focuses on corrosion, advanced players often spec into the "Restorer" branch.

*   **Skill Variants:** Later in the game, you can swap the Oxidizing Hive for a **Restorer Hive**. This replaces the corrosive drones with repair drones that automatically seek out wounded allies to replenish their armor.
*   **Stat Priority:** For a dedicated Medic build, look for gear with **Skill Power** and **Repair Skills** attributes. This increases the potency of your Chemical Trap's healing and the speed at which your drones repair armor.
*   **Weapon Synergy:** Weapons with the "Talented" or "Reformation" talents are highly recommended. These talents often trigger skill-based buffs upon landing headshots or getting kills, further boosting your healing output.

> **Warning:** Corrosive areas created by your Chemical Trap can obscure vision for your teammates. Be mindful of where you place them so you don't accidentally block a sniper's line of sight.

## Tactical Gameplay Strategies

Operating as **the division resurgence medic** requires high situational awareness. You need to track your teammates' armor bars while simultaneously identifying which enemies need to be debuffed.

### Support the Frontline
If you have a teammate playing the Vanguard specialization, your job is to stay slightly behind them. Throw your Oxidizing Hive onto them. As they engage enemies with their shield and SMG, your micro-drones will automatically fly from their position to the targets they are hitting. This adds a massive amount of "passive" DPS to the team's output.

### Area Denial
In defensive objectives, use the Chemical Trap to coat the ground in front of enemy spawn doors. This forces the AI to run through corrosive gas, stripping their armor before they even reach your team's cover line. In 2026, the AI in *The Division Resurgence* has been tuned to avoid these zones, effectively allowing you to "herd" enemies into your team's kill zone.

### Managing the Signature Ability
The Oxidizing Swarm is a "fire and forget" ability, but its placement is key. Because the drones have a travel time, try to deploy the Swarm in the center of a room. This minimizes the distance the drones have to fly to reach peripheral targets, maximizing the duration of the corrosion effect.

## FAQ

**Q: Can the Field Medic be played as a solo specialization?**

A: Yes. While it is designed for group play, the ability to attach the Oxidizing Hive to yourself makes it very strong for solo players. The corrosive damage helps solo players take down "tanky" bosses that would otherwise be difficult to defeat.

**Q: Does the Field Medic have any pure healing skills?**

A: By default, the Field Medic uses hybrid skills like the Chemical Trap (which heals slightly). However, as you level up the specialization, you can unlock the **Restorer Hive**, which is a dedicated high-output healing tool.

**Q: What is the difference between the Oxidizing Hive and the Oxidizing Swarm?**

A: The Oxidizing Hive is a standard skill with a shorter cooldown that targets specific enemies hit by you or an ally. The Oxidizing Swarm is a Signature Ability that is much more powerful, targeting all enemies in a large area simultaneously with a more potent corrosive substance.

**Q: What are the best secondary stats for the division resurgence medic gear?**

A: You should prioritize **Skill Haste** to get your Hives and Traps back faster, and **Skill Power** to increase the damage and healing values of those devices. In 2026, many players also find **Status Effect** duration to be very helpful for keeping the corrosion debuff on enemies for longer periods.

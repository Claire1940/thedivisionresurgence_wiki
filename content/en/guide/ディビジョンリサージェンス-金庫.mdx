## FAQ

**Q: Where can I find the most ディビジョンリサージェンス 金庫 in the early game?**

A: The Chelsea and Hudson Yards districts contain several apartment complexes with "Locked Safes." These are accessible early and provide a great head start on Superior (Purple) gear.

**Q: Do the contents of a safe scale with my level?**

A: Yes. Most world safes and mission-based vaults scale to your current SHD level. However, Faction Vaults in specific high-level zones will have a minimum level requirement for the loot inside.

**Q: I found a ディビジョンリサージェンス 金庫 but I don't have a key. Will it stay there if I leave?**

A: Yes, the safe will remain in that location. You can mark the spot on your map, go find a key or lockpick, and return later. However, if you log out or change world instances, the guard NPCs might respawn.

**Q: Can I share loot from a vault with my teammates?**

A: Items found in the open world are generally instanced to the individual player. However, if you are in a Co-op session, you can "drop" certain items from your inventory for your teammates to pick up within a limited timeframe after looting.

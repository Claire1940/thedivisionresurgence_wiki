## Essential First Steps for New Agents

Before you fire your first shot in Manhattan, there are several critical decisions to make. Unlike casual mobile shooters, *The Division Resurgence* features a persistent world where server choice and performance settings directly impact your competitive edge.

1.  **Server Selection:** Your character is locked to the region you choose (North America, Europe, or Asia). Cross-platform play between iOS, Android, and PC emulators is supported, but cross-server play is not. Ensure your squad settles on a single region before completing the tutorial.
2.  **Performance vs. Visuals:** In a tactical shooter, frame rate is king. Prioritize **60 FPS** over high-resolution textures. Lowering input latency will save your life in the Dark Zone more often than sharp grass textures will.
3.  **The Specialization Choice:** After the tutorial, you must pick a class. While this feels like a permanent choice, the game allows you to swap specializations at the Base of Operations later.

> **⚠️ Warning:** If you plan to play on a monitor via a USB-C hub, ensure your device supports HDMI output and power delivery. Some devices may require an HDMI splitter to bypass HDCP protections for a smooth experience.

## The Power Score System Explained

In *The Division Resurgence*, your effectiveness is measured by your **Power Score** rather than just your character level. This score is an aggregate of your gear attributes, weapon levels, and mod quality. If a mission recommends a Power Score of 15,000 and you enter with 12,000, you will find enemies significantly harder to stagger and kill.

### Components of Power Score:
*   **Gear & Weapon Levels:** The raw stats of your equipped items.
*   **Mod Quality:** Attachments for weapons and armor that provide percentage-based buffs.
*   **Account Achievements:** Global milestones that provide permanent, small stat increases.
*   **Specialization Points:** Investments in your class-specific talent tree.

> **💡 Tip:** Don't hoard gear based on rarity alone. A well-rolled "Blue" item with attributes that match your damage type is often better than a "Purple" item with irrelevant stats.

## Master the Weapon Damage Types

A core part of any **the division resurgence 攻略** involves understanding the rock-paper-scissors mechanic of damage types. As you reach level 20 and beyond, enemies will deploy specific shields that require matching damage types to break efficiently.

*   **Red (Explosive):** Highly effective against clustered enemies and specific armor plates.
*   **Light Blue (Shred):** Designed to strip away standard energy shields.
*   **Dark Blue (Penetration):** Essential for bypassing heavy physical armor on "Tank" enemies.

Late-game bosses will cycle through these shield types. Carrying a loadout that covers at least two different damage types is vital for maintaining DPS during these phases. You can find more details on weapon stats on the [official Ubisoft The Division Resurgence site](https://www.ubisoft.com/en-us/game/the-division/resurgence).

## Dark Zone Survival Tactics

The Dark Zone (DZ) is a lawless, contaminated area where the best loot resides. It is a PvPvE environment where other players can turn "Rogue" and kill you for your gear.

### Key DZ Rules:
1.  **Contaminated Loot:** Items found in the DZ must be extracted via helicopter. If you die before extraction, you lose these items.
2.  **The 20-Minute Window:** You have a limited time to scavenge and reach an extraction point.
3.  **Faction Priority:** Always prioritize the **Freemen** faction. They are currently the most dangerous AI enemies, utilizing advanced drones and heavy machinery that can wipe a squad in seconds.

## Managing Daily Progression

The game employs a daily XP bonus system to prevent burnout and level gating. Keep an eye on the orange arrow next to your level icon. When it turns gray, your XP gain rate drops significantly. To maximize your efficiency, follow this daily loop:

1.  Complete all **Daily Missions** first for the highest XP-per-minute.
2.  Progress through the **Main Campaign** until you hit a Power Score wall.
3.  Clear 10 **Open World Events** to maximize the daily bonus.
4.  Farm the **Dark Zone** or **Challenge Dens** for gear upgrades once your XP bonus is depleted.

> **💡 Tip:** Dismantling gear is usually better than selling it. You need **Worn Metal Components** and **Worn Fabric** for high-end crafting. Selling items for credits provides diminishing returns compared to the value of crafting materials.

## The Division Resurgence 攻略: Combat Tips

*   **Cover-to-Cover Movement:** Never sprint in the open. Hold the move button while looking at a new piece of cover to perform a tactical maneuver that reduces your hit box and maintains your momentum.
*   **Environmental Hazards:** Look for red barrels or gas canisters. These can clear a group of "Rikers" or "Cleaners" faster than a full magazine of ammo.
*   **Weak Points:** Heavy enemies always have a glowing yellow weak point (usually a backpack or fuel tank). Destroying these triggers a status effect like "Blind" or "Burn," making the enemy vulnerable.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the division resurgence 攻略 applicable to the PC version?**

A: Yes. Since the game supports cross-progression and cross-play, the core mechanics, specializations, and Power Score systems remain identical across mobile and PC emulators.

**Q: Can I play the entire game solo?**

A: Most of the campaign and open-world activities can be completed solo. However, high-level Challenge Dens and Dark Zone extractions are significantly easier and more rewarding when played with a coordinated squad.

**Q: What is the best way to increase Power Score quickly?**

A: Focus on upgrading your weapons first, as damage output is the primary gatekeeper for content. Secondly, ensure your armor mods are leveled up, as they provide the largest percentage boosts to your overall score.

**Q: How do I deal with Rogue players in the Dark Zone?**

A: Never trust a solo player loitering near an extraction zone. If you see a player's name turn red, they have gone Rogue. Use cover, deploy your signature skills immediately, and try to fight near AI enemies who may also target the Rogue player.

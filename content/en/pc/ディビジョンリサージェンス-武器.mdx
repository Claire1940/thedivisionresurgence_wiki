## A-Tier: Reliable Workhorses for World Tier 5

While S-Tier weapons are the goal, A-Tier **ディビジョンリサージェンス 武器** are excellent alternatives that can reliably clear World Tier 5 content and most Incursions. These guns often have slightly lower fire rates or more specific handling requirements but remain highly competitive.

*   **ACR:** This assault rifle rivals the M4 in terms of handling and stability. While its fire rate is slightly lower, its predictable recoil makes it a favorite for players who prefer mid-range precision.
*   **Vector 45 ACP:** Known for its blistering rate of fire, the Vector can melt enemies at close range almost instantly. However, its small magazine requires frequent reloading, making "Extended Mag" mods mandatory.
*   **Custom M44:** A high-damage bolt-action sniper preferred by players who want the "one-tap" potential in the Dark Zone. It scales exceptionally well with Firearms stats.
*   **Big Alejandro:** An exotic LMG that grants increased damage and fire rate while in cover. It is the undisputed king for defensive playstyles and suppressing enemy factions like the Freemen.

## Essential Weapon Talents for 2026

In the world of Division Resurgence, raw damage is only half the story. To truly master your **ディビジョンリサージェンス 武器**, you must understand how talents interact with your core stats: Firearms, Toughness, and Electronics.

### Top PvE Talents
For Incursions like "Dragon's Nest" or "Falcon Lost," focus on talents that shred armor and sustain your health:
1.  **Destructive:** Increases enemy armor damage. Since almost all endgame enemies are heavily armored, this is a non-negotiable requirement for high-level play.
2.  **Ferocious:** Boosts damage against Elite and Named enemies, making boss fights significantly shorter.
3.  **Predatory:** Killing a target regenerates health over time. This is the premier survival talent for solo players.

### Top PvP Talents
In the Dark Zone, the priority shifts toward burst damage and utility. Use the table below to optimize your PvP loadout.

| Talent | Effect | Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Responsive** | Damage increases when close to the target | Essential for SMG rushers and shotgun builds. |
| **Unforgiving** | Damage increases as your health drops | A high-risk, high-reward talent for winning 1v1 duels. |
| **Competent** | Weapon damage increases after using a skill | Pairs perfectly with Pulse or First Aid for a burst of power. |

## How to Farm Top-Tier Weapons in 2026

Acquiring the best **ディビジョンリサージェンス 武器** requires a strategic approach to farming. In 2026, the most efficient methods involve high-difficulty activities and time-limited events.

*   **Global Events:** These monthly occurrences allow you to earn GE Tokens, which can be traded for Classified Gear Caches and Exotic Weapon Caches.
*   **Legendary Missions:** Completing missions like "Amherst's Apartment" on Legendary difficulty guarantees an Exotic drop once per week.
*   **Dark Zone Extraction:** Clearing landmarks in DZ07-DZ09 offers the highest density of High-End drops, provided you can extract them safely.
*   **Resistance Mode:** This horde-based mode is widely considered the best way to farm "The House" and other top-tier exotics by opening reward crates after Wave 10.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not ignore lower Gear Score weapons if they have perfect talents. You can use the "Optimization Station" at the Base of Operations to raise a "God Roll" weapon to its maximum level later.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the single best weapon in Division Resurgence right now?**

A: While it depends on your build, the **Lightweight M4** is widely considered the most versatile **ディビジョンリサージェンス 武器** due to its high fire rate and predictable recoil. For SMG enthusiasts, **The House** is the undisputed champion.

**Q: How do I reroll bad talents on my weapons?**

A: Visit the "Recalibration Station" in the Base of Operations. You can spend Weapon Parts and Phoenix Credits to change one talent on a High-End weapon. Once you change a talent, the other two are locked, so choose wisely.

**Q: Is Gear Score more important than weapon talents?**

A: No. A lower Gear Score weapon with perfect talents (like Destructive and Ferocious) will almost always outperform a max Gear Score weapon with useless talents. Focus on the quality of the roll rather than the number.

**Q: Can I get Exotic weapons outside of the Dark Zone?**

A: Yes. In the 2026 meta, Exotics can be obtained through Weekly Caches, Legendary Mission rewards, Resistance crates, and even Open World bosses. The Dark Zone is simply one of the fastest—and most dangerous—methods.

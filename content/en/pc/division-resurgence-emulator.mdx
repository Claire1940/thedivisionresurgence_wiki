## In-Game Graphics Optimization

Once your emulator is configured, you must balance the in-game settings. The Division Resurgence uses a dynamic scaling system that can sometimes conflict with emulator resolution overrides.

1.  **Frame Rate:** Cap this at **60 FPS** within the game. While the emulator might support 120Hz, the game's physics engine is most stable at 60.
2.  **Resolution:** Set to **High**. If you notice input lag, drop this to Standard before lowering your graphics quality.
3.  **Advanced Effects:** 
    *   **Shadow Quality:** Low (Saves significant GPU cycles).
    *   **Anti-Aliasing:** On (Crucial to prevent pixelated edges on large monitors).
    *   **Bloom:** Off (Reduces visual clutter during explosions).
    *   **Volumetric Lighting:** Off (Improves visibility in foggy environments).

> **⚠️ Warning:** Avoid using "Dynamic Resolution" in the advanced settings. It can cause the screen to blur unexpectedly during combat as the emulator struggles to sync the variable scaling.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

Even with a perfect setup, emulating a 2026 mobile title can present challenges. Here are the most common fixes for [The Division Resurgence official site](https://www.ubisoft.com/en-us/game/the-division/resurgence) players:

### White or Missing Character Textures
This is almost always a shader cache issue. 
*   **Fix:** Change your Graphics Engine from OpenGL to Vulcan in the emulator settings.
*   **Alternative:** Update your PC's graphics drivers to the latest April 2026 version.

### Input Lag and "Floaty" Mouse
If your aim feels delayed, it is likely due to V-Sync or high polling rates.
*   **Fix:** Disable V-Sync in both the emulator and your GPU control panel.
*   **Fix:** Set your mouse polling rate to 500Hz instead of 1000Hz; some emulators struggle with ultra-high frequency input.

### Crashing on Startup
If the game closes immediately after the Ubisoft logo:
*   **Fix:** Check if "Root Mode" is enabled in your emulator settings. Some mobile games have anti-cheat measures that block rooted devices. Turn Root **OFF**.
*   **Fix:** Ensure your "Device Model" is set to a modern flagship, such as the **Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra** or **ASUS ROG Phone**, to ensure the game downloads the correct asset packs.

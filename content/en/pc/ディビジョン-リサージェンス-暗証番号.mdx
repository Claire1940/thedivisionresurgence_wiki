## Maximizing Your Power Score with Safe Rewards

Once you have used the **ディビジョン リサージェンス 暗証番号** to unlock high-tier loot, the next step is integrating those rewards into your build. In *The Division Resurgence*, your Power Score is an aggregate of every piece of equipment you own. This includes your primary weapons, armor pieces, and even the smallest muzzle attachments.

### Understanding Core Attributes
When you pull new gear from a safe, pay attention to the three core attributes. Equipping random high-rarity gear can actually weaken your character if the stats don't align with your specialization.

*   **Firepower:** Increases raw weapon damage and critical hit chance.
*   **Toughness:** Boosts total armor and health, essential for solo play.
*   **Engineering:** Enhances skill intensity, reducing cooldowns and increasing turret/drone health.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not simply equip gear because it has a "green arrow" indicating a higher score. Check if it breaks your 4-piece Gear Set bonus, which provides massive attribute spikes.

## FAQ

**Q: Where do I find the ディビジョン リサージェンス 暗証番号 for the safe in the first hospital mission?**

A: Look for a yellow sticky note on the computer monitor in the nurse's station directly across from the locked door. If you miss it, check your Database under "Cryptic Clues" after you've touched the keypad.

**Q: Can I share safe codes with my friends in a group?**

A: Yes, the **ディビジョン リサージェンス 暗証番号** for world-based safes are static for all players. However, loot inside the safe is instanced, meaning everyone in the party gets their own individual rewards.

**Q: What should I do if a code I found in the database doesn't work?**

A: Ensure you are at the correct safe. Some missions have multiple locked containers. Check the name of the clue in your database and match it to the mission objective or the room name you are currently in.

**Q: Do I lose the items if I don't pick them up from the safe?**

A: No. Any loot left on the ground or inside an opened safe is automatically sent to your "Missed Loot" stash in the Base of Operations. You can retrieve it at any time without fear of losing progress.

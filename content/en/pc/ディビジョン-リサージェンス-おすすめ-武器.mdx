## The Division Resurgence Best Weapons Tier List (S-Tier)

### Lightweight M4 (Assault Rifle)
The Lightweight M4 remains the gold standard for versatility. With a high RPM (rounds per minute) and incredibly predictable vertical recoil, it excels in almost every scenario. It is widely considered the most balanced AR in the game, allowing for consistent headshots even at the edge of its effective range.

### The House (Exotic SMG)
For close-quarters combat, "The House" is unrivaled. Its unique talent, **Card Counter**, provides a massive damage bonus that alternates between the first and second halves of the magazine. When paired with a crit-heavy build, it can melt elite targets in a single clip.

### Warlord (Exotic Assault Rifle)
The Warlord has made a triumphant return as a top-tier **ディビジョン リサージェンス おすすめ 武器**. Its primary strength lies in its damage reduction talent, which grants the user significant survivability while firing. This makes it the premier choice for solo players tackling difficult campaign content or Dark Zone extractions.

### M700 Carbon (Marksman Rifle)
For agents who prefer long-distance engagements, the M700 Carbon offers the highest base damage in the marksman class. Its "Free Talent" slot allows for easier "God Roll" configurations, making it the preferred tool for eliminating snipers and engineers before they can deploy skills.

A-Tier weapons are excellent alternatives when you haven't yet secured an Exotic or a perfect S-Tier roll. These guns are highly effective for World Tier 5 content and most Incursions.

## Essential Weapon Talents for PvE and PvP

In *The Division Resurgence*, the raw damage number is only half the story. To truly optimize your **ディビジョン リサージェンス おすすめ 武器**, you must roll for talents that synergize with your playstyle and specialization.

### Top PvE Talents
1.  **Destructive:** Increases Armor Damage. Since almost all endgame enemies are heavily armored, this is a non-negotiable talent for high-level efficiency.
2.  **Ferocious:** Increases damage against Elites and Named enemies. Essential for boss rooms and Heroic encounters.
3.  **Predatory:** Killing a target regenerates health over time. This is the ultimate survival talent for solo players.

## How to Farm Top-Tier Weapons in 2026

Securing the best **ディビジョン リサージェンス おすすめ 武器** requires a strategic approach to farming. In the 2026 meta, certain activities offer significantly higher drop rates for high-end and exotic gear.

1.  **Heroic Strongholds:** These are the most reliable source for S-tier drops. They rotate every 24 hours, so check the rewards daily.
2.  **Resistance Mode:** This horde-based mode is widely considered the best way to farm for "The House" and other top-tier exotics. Reaching Wave 10 and opening the reward crate is the meta-standard for efficient farming.
3.  **The Dark Zone (DZ07-DZ09):** The northern zones of the Dark Zone offer the highest density of high-end loot. However, you must successfully extract these items, which carries the risk of Rogue agents.
4.  **Global Events:** Participate in monthly events to earn GE Tokens. These can be traded for Classified Gear Caches and Exotic Weapon Caches, providing a guaranteed path to power.

## FAQ

**Q: What is the single best weapon for a beginner in The Division Resurgence?**

A: The **P416** or **ACR** are the best starting choices. They have very manageable recoil and balanced stats, making them easier to use while you learn the game's mechanics and enemy patterns.

**Q: Can I change the damage type (Blast, Shredding, Piercing) of my weapon?**

A: No, the damage type is fixed upon the weapon's drop. This makes finding a high-rarity version of your favorite **ディビジョン リサージェンス おすすめ 武器** with the correct damage type the primary goal of the endgame grind.

**Q: Is Power Score more important than weapon talents?**

A: Generally, no. A weapon with a slightly lower Power Score but "God Roll" talents (like Destructive and Ferocious) will almost always outperform a max-level weapon with useless talents. You can use the Optimization Station later to bring your perfectly rolled weapon up to the maximum level.

**Q: How do I unlock Exotic weapons like the Warlord?**

A: Exotics can be obtained through the Classified Assignment Pass, as rare drops from Heroic bosses, or by opening Exotic Caches earned through Weekly Projects and Global Events. The Warlord is often featured as a milestone reward in seasonal tracks.

## Optimizing The Division Resurgence for Low-End PCs

If your current rig falls slightly below **the division resurgence pc system requirements**, there are several software-level tweaks you can implement to boost performance. Since the game is built on Unreal Engine 4, many standard UE4 optimization tricks apply here.

1.  **Resolution Scaling:** If you are struggling to maintain 30 FPS, drop the resolution scale to 85% or 90%. This provides a significant performance boost with minimal impact on visual clarity.
2.  **Shadow Quality:** This is the single most demanding setting. Lowering shadows from "High" to "Low" can often net a 15-20% increase in frame rate.
3.  **Object Detail:** Reducing the draw distance for non-essential objects will help your CPU keep up during high-speed movement through the city.
4.  **Update Drivers:** Ensure you are using the latest drivers from NVIDIA or AMD released in early 2026, as these often contain specific optimizations for Ubisoft's proprietary engine modifications.

For more information on official updates and patches, you can visit the [official Ubisoft Resurgence page](https://www.ubisoft.com/en-us/game/the-division/resurgence) to stay informed on the latest technical requirements.

2.  **Download Emulator:** Visit the official website of your chosen emulator and download the latest 64-bit version.
3.  **Configure Instance:** Create a new instance using **Android 11 (or higher)**. The Division Resurgence requires modern Android architecture to run its advanced lighting and physics.
4.  **Allocate Resources:** In the emulator settings, allocate at least **4 CPU Cores** and **8GB of RAM** to the instance.

## How to Connect Your Controller

Setting up **the division resurgence gamepad support** is a straightforward process, but it varies slightly depending on your mobile operating system. Ensure your controller is fully charged before attempting to pair it to avoid disconnection during critical missions.

### Android Setup
1. Open your device **Settings** and navigate to **Bluetooth**.
2. Put your controller into pairing mode (e.g., hold the Share and PS buttons on a DualSense).
3. Select the controller from the list of available devices.
4. Launch The Division Resurgence; the game will prompt you with a controller tutorial upon entry.

### iOS Setup
1. Go to **Settings** > **Bluetooth** and ensure it is toggled on.
2. Activate pairing mode on your gamepad.
3. Tap the device name under "Other Devices."
4. Once "Connected" appears, open the game. You can verify the connection in the in-game settings menu.

## Troubleshooting Common Gamepad Issues

If you find that your **the division resurgence gamepad support** is not functioning correctly, follow these common fixes to get back into the action:

1. **Unresponsive Buttons:** This often happens if the controller was connected *after* the game was launched. Completely close the app, ensure the controller is paired in your phone's OS, and then restart the game.
2. **Incorrect Key Prompts:** If you are using a PlayStation controller but see Xbox prompts, check the "Controller Type" setting in the menu. In some beta versions, this may default to Xbox icons.
3. **Ghosting/Lag:** This is typically caused by interference with other Bluetooth devices. Try turning off Bluetooth on nearby tablets or computers to clear the signal.
4. **Firmware Updates:** Modern controllers like the DualSense and Xbox Series controllers require firmware updates. Connect them to a console or PC to ensure they are running the latest software for maximum mobile compatibility.
